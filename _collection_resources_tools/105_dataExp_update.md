<p>
      <h3>Keep your dataset current and accurately documented with the <i>Update My Dataset</i> workflow.</h3>
 </p>

<p>
 <div class="survey-link">
  <a href="https://clinepidb.org/ce/app/workspace/datasets/new">Update My Dataset</a>
</div>
 </p>
Click the <i>Update My Dataset</i> button to open the <i>My Dataset Properties</i> table. From there, select the dataset you wish to open in the Update My Dataset workflow.

<b>The Update My Dataset workflow allows you to </b>
<li>
<ul>delete the dataset</ul>
<ul>replace the data file</ul>
<ul>update the associated variable annotations file</ul>
<ul>revise dataset-level metadata details</ul>
<ul>upload other supporting files</ul>
</li>

All changes you make will be reflected immediately in the <i>Exploratory Data Analysis</i> workspace the next time you open the dataset.
