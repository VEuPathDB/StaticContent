<p><b>dataExplorer</b> is a <b>self-service data platform</b> that helps you move from curiosity to insight. Using intuitive, browser-based tools, you can upload, explore, visualize, and analyze your own datasets, without specialized infrastructure or software. You control access to your data, share it with collaborators or the wider community in a FAIR-aligned manner, and discover datasets shared by others. </p>

<p>Built on the proven interactive bioinformatic tools of VEuPathDB and ClinEpiDB, dataExplorer extends these capabilities to support <b>flexible, do-it-yourself data upload and sharing</b>.</p>
