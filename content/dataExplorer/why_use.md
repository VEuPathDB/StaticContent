  <h1>Why Use dataExplorer</h1>
  <div>
  <p>dataExplorer is part of the VEuPathDB family of databases, backed by more than 25 years of experience building powerful web-based data resources for the global research community.</p>

  <p>VEuPathDB’s websites provide access to genomics, epidemiological, and microbiome data alongside powerful bioinformatics tools used by researchers worldwide to drive discovery.</p>

  <p>dataExplorer extends this ecosystem by making it easy to upload, explore, share, and reuse datasets in a secure and collaborative environment.</p>
  </div>

  <h2>Upload your dataset</h2>
  <div>
  <p>Quickly and privately turn spreadsheets into insight using dataExplorer’s interactive analysis tools, while ensuring your dataset is preserved for long-term stewardship.</p>

  <p><b>Preserve your data</b>: Ensure your dataset and associated documentation remain accessible and well organized over time, with version controlled updates and stable identifiers that support clear citation.</p>

  <p><b>Discover patterns faster</b>: Reveal trends, distributions, and relationships within your data that are difficult to see in spreadsheets. Histograms, frequency tables, and summary statistics are automatically generated for each variable upon upload, allowing you to quickly understand data distributions and identify patterns at a glance.</p>

  <p><b>Build visualizations in seconds</b>: Create plots and maps with simple point-and-click tools…no coding required!</p>

  <p><b>Explore your dataset privately</b>: Your dataset will remain completely private unless you choose to share it.</p>
  </div>

<h2>Share your dataset</h2>
  <div>
  <p>Sharing your dataset through dataExplorer increases its visibility, usability, and long-term value while allowing you to maintain control over access decisions. This enables others to build on your work and extend the impact of your research.</p>

  <p><b>Retain ownership while controlling access to your data</b>: Choose whether your dataset is private, shared directly with collaborators, or shared broadly with the wider community.</p>

  <p><b>Collaborate more easily</b>: Allow labmates and collaborators to securely explore datasets and analyses through a shared online workspace.</p>

  <p><b>Meet (and exceed) funder and journal requirements</b>: Comply with data availability and data management policies by providing structured dataset documentation and data access through a trusted database.</p>

  <p><b>Increase visibility</b>: Publishing datasets on a widely used research platform makes your work easier to discover and promotes new collaborations.</p>

  <p><b>Maximize value</b>: Support secondary analyses that may lead to new discoveries. Increase the reach and impact of your research investment by enabling others to download, reuse, and cite your contextualized dataset.</p>
  </div>

<h2>Explore datasets from other researchers</h2>
  <div>
  <p>dataExplorer allows you to rapidly discover and evaluate datasets contributed by the research community.</p>

  <p><b>Accelerate discovery</b>: Search, explore, download, and reuse datasets shared by other researchers.</p>

  <p><b>Save time and resources</b>: Leverage existing data rather than collecting new datasets from scratch.</p>

  <p>Assess datasets before downloading</b>: Explore which variables were collected and review data completeness and summary statistics to determine whether a dataset fits your research needs.</p>

  <p><b>Benchmark and validate results</b>: Compare your findings with established datasets to support reproducibility and validation.</p>

  <p><b>Teach with real-world data</b>: Use real research datasets to teach analysis methods, statistics, and data science skills.</p>
  </div>
