    <h4><a href="https://fungidb.org">FungiDB</a> User Comments and Gene Annotation Campaign - Now through March 22, 2026</h4>
  </div>

  <div class="contents">
    <div class="workshop">
      <p style="color: maroon;">We’re <b>thrilled to invite you</b> to the FungiDB User Comments and Gene Annotation Campaign, happening alongside the 33rd <b>33rd Fungal Genetics Conference</b> in Asilomar, USA. This year, we’re supercharging participation with **AI-powered tools** to make paper curation and user comment submission faster and easier than ever.</p>
      
      <h4>Why Your Contributions Matter!</h4>
      <ul>
        <li><b>Enhance genome annotations:</b> Provide crucial evidence like alternative gene models, knockout phenotypes, subcellular localization, and PubMed references.</li> 
        <li><b>Make gene data more accessible:</b> Help researchers worldwide discover and use reliable gene information.</li> 
        <li><b>Support the scientific community:</b> Keep gene annotations up-to-date, accurate, and trustworthy.</li> 
      </ul>
   
      <h4>How to Participate:</h4> 
      <ol> 
        <li>Leave a user comment on a gene record page OR improve gene annotations directly using the <b>Apollo genome editor</b>.</li> 
        <li>Stop by the FungiDB help desk during the ECFG17 poster sessions to claim your <b>free "Got Fungus?" mug</b>! The <b>top 10 contributors</b> will also receive an exclusive T-shirt.</li> 
      </ol> 
      <p><b>Not attending?</b> No problem! Submit your comments online and have a colleague pick up your mug for you.</p>

      <h4>Submitting a User Comment</h4> 
      <ul> 
        <li>Sign in or <a href="https://fungidb.org/fungidb/app/user/registration">register at FungiDB.org</a>.</li> 
        <li>Navigate to the relevant gene record page.</li> 
        <li>Click <b>"Add a user comment"</b> and fill out the simple form.</li> </ul>
      <img class="image-frame" style="width: 30%; margin-left: 4em;"  src="{{ "/assets/images/fungidb/ECFG17uc.png" | absolute_url }}" alt="User Comment Form"/>

      <h4>What to Include in a User Comment</h4> 
      <p>Share any new gene information, such as:</p> 
      <ul> 
        <li>Gene names or synonyms (e.g., <i>Purine Phosphoribosyl Transferase, also known as HPRT, HGPRT</i>)</li> 
        <li>PubMed IDs</li> <li>Functional characterization</li> 
        <li>Corrections to gene model predictions or annotations</li> 
        <li>Phenotype data and other experimental evidence</li> 
      </ul> 
          
      <h4>Bulk Submissions Made Easy</h4> 
      <p>Have many comments? No problem! Use our <a href="https://tinyurl.com/commentsfungi">bulk submission form</a> and send it to us via the <a href="https://fungidb.org/fungidb/app/contact-us">Contact Us page</a>.</p> 
      <p><b>Need Help?</b> Reach out anytime: <a href="https://fungidb.org/fungidb/app/contact-us">Contact Us</a>.</p>
