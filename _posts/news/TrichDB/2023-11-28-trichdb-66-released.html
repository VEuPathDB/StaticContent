New data in {{project}} 66

Transcriptomics

  • Trichomonas vaginalis response to metronidazole – RNA seq transcriptome of T vaginalis in normal culture conditions and treated with metronidazole at 25 µg/ml for 3 h. Xie et al. 2023
  • Trichomonas vaginalis transcriptome response to apicidin (HDAC inhibitor) – RNA seq transcriptome of Trichomonas vaginalis in normal culture conditions or treated with 70 nM apicidin, a class I/II HDAC inhibitor, for 4 hours. Song et al. 2017

ChIP-Seq

  • Localization histone H3 modifications (H3K4me3 and H3K27Ac) – ChIP-Seq of Trichomonas vaginalis using antibodies against H3K4me3 and H3K27Ac. Farhat et al. 2020

Proteomics

  • Trichomonasvirus effect on T. vag extracellular vesicle proteome – Proteomes of exosomes derived from virus-infected cells or controls: clones TV79-49c1+, and its virus-free derivative TV79-49c1-. Rada et al. 2022

New features in {{project}} 66

  • New options for downloading gene features are now availalbe from any gene record page. Click the Download Gene link at the top of the record page to use the new BED and FASTA download options.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
