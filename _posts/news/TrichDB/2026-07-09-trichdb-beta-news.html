New Features

MAJOR UPDATES IN TrichDB 70
  • VEuPathDB Searches now have a new feature: Organism Presets allow you to save and apply a selection of organisms across various searches. Select a set of organisms you commonly use and click 'Save'. Then, in any search with an organism parameter, you can apply your saved selection. Additionally, there is a new option to expand the tree to show selections.
  • VEuPathDB gene product descriptions have been updated using the UniProt ARBA rules (https://www.uniprot.org/help/arba). For genes with uninformative descriptions (such as “hypothetical protein” or “unspecified protein”), information from InterPro and taxonomy is used to generate a description based on properties such as function, catalytic activity, pathway membership, subcellular location, or the presence of a Pfam domain. In build 70, the new VEuPath gene annotation pipeline has replaced over 2 million "hypothetical" gene descriptions with more informative functional annotation!
  • The My Datasets feature continues to expand, making it easier for users to upload, explore, and share their own data within VEuPathDB. This release includes several enhancements:
    • Dataset sharing: Users can now share the datasets they have uploaded. Datasets can be shared privately with specific collaborators, or made public to support open data access and to meet funder and journal data-sharing requirements.
    • Improved upload experience: The redesigned upload interface now allows users to provide meaningful metadata to describe and contextualize their uploaded dataset. Metadata is required for datasets that will be made public.
    • New data type: Users can now upload a Data Table, a simple tabular dataset (such as a spreadsheet or CSV file) containing any type of data, for private exploration or public sharing.
    • Enhanced upload validation: Improved validation for RNA-Seq and bigWig files helps identify issues before upload.
    • Expanded upload guidance: New help content makes it easier to prepare and upload datasets.
  • VEuPathDB has launched dataExplorer, a brand new platform designed for quick data exploration and visualization of data tables from any domain! Users can keep their uploaded datasets private, share datasets with specific people, or make the dataset public to fulfill data sharing requirements. Read more about it here.
  • Please send us your feedback and bug reports using our Contact Us form or by emailing us at help@veupathdb.org
