New data in FungiDB 56

Genome Sequence and Annotation: updated genomes

  • Trichoderma reesei QM6a 2017 – Wan-Chen Li et al. 2017.

Genome Sequence and Annotation:

  • Candida dubliniensis CD36 – Jackson et al. 2009.
  • Candida metapsilosis BP57 – Mixão et al. 2019.
  • Cladophialophora bantiana CBS 173.52 – Teixeira et al. 2017.
  • Cyberlindnera fabianii 65 – van Rijswijck et al. 2017.
  • Fusarium oxysporum f. sp. conglutinans Fo5176 – Fokkens et al. 2020.
  • Lodderomyces elongisporus NRRL YB-4239 – Butler et al. 2009.
  • Meyerozyma guilliermondii ATCC 6260 – Seixas et al. 2019.
  • Pneumocystis oryctolagi CS1 – Cissé et al. 2021.
  • Pneumocystis wakefieldiae 2A – Cissé et al. 2021.
  • Trichomonascus ciferrii CBS 4856 – Mixão et al. 2019.
  • Trichophyton rubrum CBS 118892 – Martinez et al. 2012.

Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis:

  • Aspergillus fumigatus
    • Whole Genome Association Study for Itraconazole Sensitivity in Aspergillus fumigatus – Clinical isolates of A. fumigatus from Japan. Zhao et al. 2021.
    • Strain specific A. fumigatus persistence in the murine lung – Characterisation of W72310 strain by WGS and comparison to a common reference strain, CEA10 Jones et al. 2021.
  • Candida albicans
    • Candida albicans adaptation to azole antifungal drugs – A study of the rapid adaptation to antifungal drugs. Todd et al. 2020.
  • Candida auris
    • C. auris B11220 mono- and multidrug resistance evolution experiment – Experimentally evolved MDR clade II C. auris. Carolus et al. 2021.
  • Cryptococcus neoformans
    • Genetic basis of within-host microevolution underlying recurrent Cryptococcal meningitis infection – Whole-genome sequencing assessing the genetic basis of Cryptococcal infection in 17 HIV-infected individuals with recurrent cryptococcal meningitis. Rhodes et al. 2017.

Transcriptomics

  • Coccidioides immitis
    • Transcriptional analysis of Coccidioides immitis mycelia and spherules — Analysis of gene expression changes in mycelia and young and mature spherules. Carlin et al. 2021.
  • Fusarium fujikuroi
    • In planta expression profiles of Fusarium fujikuroi — Transcriptome of the fungi in infected maize roots growing in CM agar. Niehaus et al. 2016.
  • Mucor circinelloides
    • Transcriptomics of Mucor circinelloides NCRIP deficient spores phagocytosed by mouse macrophages — Transcriptomics of R3B2 and RDRP1 fungal spores and fungal spore-macrophage coculture. This data set was re-aligned against the genome of Mucor lusitanicus CBS 277.49 and now also contains the control sample L15 R7B 5h. Pérez-Arques et al. 2020.
  • Neurospora crassa
    • Comparative transcriptome of Neurospora crassa WT and delta mus52 strains — RNA-pool of both species extracted at 0min (control), 15, 30, 60, 120 and 240 min. Martins et al. 2018.
    • Neurospora crassa gene co-expression network — The co-expression network was used to determine the transcriptional response of Neurospora crassa to various carbohydrates. Philipp Benz et al. 2021 (submitted).

Phenotype data

  • Manually curated Phenotypes (VEuPathDB) – We have curated and integrated phenotypes from Valero et al. 2020 for Aspergillus fumigatus Af293 and Saito et al. 2012 for Pyricularia oryzae 70-15 in this release.

Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
