VEuPathDB named as an Elixir Core Data Resource

Inducted in December 2023, VEuPathDB is honored to join the select group of Elixir Core Data Resources (CDR). With this distinction, VEuPathDB is identified as a resource of fundamental importance to the life science community and the preservation of biological data, dedicated to the principle of open access for publicly funded research. The CDR program recognizes resources that promote excellence and provide infrastructure vital for the advancement of the life sciences. Evaluation criteria include scientific focus, quality of science and community service, governance, impact, and ability to facilitate scientific research.

    Transcriptomics

    • Candida parapsilosis
      • Transcription profiles of three clinical isolates – Three clinical isolates exhibiting adhesive phenotypes were incubated at 37°C in YPD, M199, or pooled human plasma. Bliss et al. 2021.
    • Coccidioides posadasii
      • Transcriptome profiling of Coccidioides posadasii WT and CPS1 deletion mutant – Transcriptomes of WT and the avirulent cps-1 mutant. Narra et al. 2016.
    • Neurospora crassa
      • Gene expression during conidia germination on PDA and Bird medium – mRNA were sampled and compared from four time points across conida germination in Neurpsora crassa on different media. Lopez-Giraldez et al. 2022.
      • A role for gene expression and mRNA stability in nutritional compensation of the circadian clock – 3' End Sequencing was performed on biological duplicate samples from 25C, constant light cultures grown for 72 hours to identify poly(A) tail locations. Kelliher et al. 2023.
    • Parastagonospora nodorum
      • In planta and in vitro RNA-Seq profiling of Parastagonospora nodorum – In planta and in vitro RNA sequencing of Parastagonospora nodorum SN15 and a knockout mutant lacking the transcription factor Pf-2. Jones et al. 2019.

    Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis

    • Candida auris
      • SNP calls on WGS of isolates collected by NY State Dept. of Health (Wadsworth Center) – Genomic data for 481 isolates. CDC, 2016.
    • Clavispora lusitaniae
      • SNP calls on WGS of bloodstream isolates – Whole genome sequencing of Clavispora lusitaniae clinical bloodstream isolates. Scott et al. 2023.
    • Saccharomyces cerevisiae
      • SNP calls on WGS of strains from the National Collection of Yeast Culture (Norwich, U.K.) – Genomic data for 230 strains. Quadram Institute.

    New features in {{project}} 67

    • New options for downloading gene features are now available from any gene record page. Click the Download Gene link at the top of the record page to use the new BED and FASTA download options.


    Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
