Transcriptomics

  • Candida albicans
    • A study of MBF1 and GCN4 roles on C. albicans gene expression – In vitro and in vivo transcriptomic investigation of MBF1 role in C. albicans virulence. Amorim-Vaz et al. 2021.
    • Investigation of mdivi-1-driven metabolic reprogramming and levels of endogenous nitric oxide (NO) on gene expression. – Investigating the role of small molecule mitochondrial fission inhibitor MDIVI-1. Koch et al. 2018.
    • Transcriptome dynamics in Candida and human cell culture during infection. &ndash Characterization of the host cell and C. albicans transcriptomes during in vitro infection of human endothelial or oral epithelial cells. Liu et al. 2015.
  • Candida auris
    • Fresh whole blood infected with Candida auris – Candida transcriptome was studied using ex vivo whole blood infection model. Allert et al. 2022.
  • Pyricularia oryzae
    • Comparative transcriptomic analysis of appressorium development in Magnaporthe oryzae – Transcriptional profiles during appressorium development. Osés-Ruiz et al. 2022.
  • Rhizopus delemar
    • In vitro infections of mouse BMDMs with Rhizopus delemar – Mouse bone-marrow derived macrophages (BMDMs) were infected the pathogenic mold, Rhizopus delemar, for 1, 4 or 18 hours and then subject to RNA-seq analysis to determine the transcriptional response of both the fungus and the host to infection. Andrianaki et al. 2018.

New features in {{project}} 65

  • VEuPathDB Galaxy now offers a preconfigured ChIP Seq workflow. This workflow processes ChIP Seq FASTQ files by aligning to your chosen reference genome with BWA then calling peaks using MACS2. Peaks calculated from the input FASTQ files are converted to bigWig format which can be exported from Galaxy to your VEuPathDB account.
  • Strategy Improvement – A new option in the gene result table provides a view of search specific data columns for your preferred subset of genes. Click the 'Show only the Genes in my basket' option from any gene result to filter the result based on the genes in your basket. This can be used in combination with the 'Send to Basket (replace)' option to view search specific data columns for multi-step strategy results. Filter by Basket tutorial.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
