New data in FungiDB 55

Genome Sequence and Annotation:

  • Arthrobotrys flagrans CBS H-5679 – Youssar et al. 2019.
  • Candida glabrata BG2 – Xu et al. 2021.
  • Coprinopsis cinerea #326 – Xie et al. 2021.
  • Debaryomyces hansenii CBS767 – Dujon et al. 2014.
  • Diutina rugosa CBS 613 – Mixão et al. 2019.
  • Emergomyces pasteurianus Ep9510 – Dukik et al. 2017.
  • Pneumocystis canis CanA – Cissé et al. 2021.
  • Pneumocystis canis P2C – Cissé et al. 2021.
  • Phytophthora capsici Strain LT1534 (2021) – Stajich et al. 2021.
  • Taphrina deformans PYCC 5710 – Cissé et al. 2013.

Genome Sequence:

  • Lichtheimia ramosa KPH11 – Jeong-Ah Seo et al. 2019.
  • Apophysomyces variabilis BNCCPF 102052 – Prakash et al. 2017.

Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis:

  • Candida auris
    • SNP calls on WGS of Candida auris isolates from the coastal wetlands of Andaman Islands, India – 13 Candida auris strains isolated in the present study from the South Andaman district, Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Union Territory of India. Aurora et al. 2021.
    • SNP calls on WGS of laboratory evolved C. auris strains – Experimental evolution assays using a fluconazole-susceptible isolate of C. auris (BJCA001). Bing et al. 2020.
  • Candida albicans
    • SNP calls on WGS of Candida albicans collected from old oaks and NCYC 597 clinical strain – Short-read genome data were generated for the type strain of C. albicans (NCYC 597) and three strains isolated from the bark of oak trees in the New Forest in the United Kingdom. Bensasson et al. 2019.
    • SNP calls on WGS of 182 isolates of Candida albicans collected worldwide – Isolates were collected worldwide, and include commensal isolates, as well as ones responsible for superficial and invasive infections. Ropars et al. 2018.
  • Fusarium graminearum
    • SNP calls on WGS of six F. graminearum strains collected from wheat fields in France – Sequencing analysis of F. graminearum strains collected in France. Ropars et al. 2017.

Transcriptomics

  • Candida albicans
    • Expression profiles of fluconazole resistant and fluconazole sensitive clinical isolates — The expression profiles of fluconazole resistant (U) and fluconazole sensitive (L) clinical isolates, from the same patient, all containing mutation in the trancription factor Mrr1. Demers et al. 2018.
  • Aspergillus fumigatus
    • Gene expression in WT, hrmA deletion, hrmA OE, hrmA_REV, EVOL under hypoxia and normoxia conditions — Gene expression differences in Aspergillus fumigatus wild type, hrmA mutant, and overexpression under hypoxia and normoxia conditions. Kowalski et al. 2019.

Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
