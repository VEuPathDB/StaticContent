New data in FungiDB 66

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Chaetothyriales sp. CBS 132003 – Westerdijk Fungal Biodiversity Institute.
  • Leptosphaeria maculans JN3 – Rouxel et al. 2012.

Genome replacements

  • Coccidioides immitis H538.4 – Neafsey et al. 2010.
  • Malassezia globosa CBS 7966 – Xu et al. 2007.
  • Rhizophagus irregularis DAOM 197198 – Yildirir et al. 2021.

Genome notes

  • Podosphaera aphanis DRCT72020 – Warning: contaminated genome. We are working to reload this genome using the latest NCBI version GCA_022627015.2.

Annotation Updates (Product Descriptions)

  • Pfam domain names were substituted for product descriptions for genes with assigned Pfam domains and uninformative product descriptions such as 'unspecified product' for the following organisms: Aspergillus versicolor CBS 583.65, Puccinia striiformis strain 93-210, Blumeria graminis f. sp. tritici 96224, Blumeria graminis f. sp. triticale THUN-12, Aspergillus carbonarius ITEM 5010, Aspergillus wentii DTO 134E9, and Aspergillus aculeatus ATCC 16872.

Transcriptomics

  • Aspergillus fumigatus
    • Transcriptomics of Aspergillus fumigatus strains upon exposure to human airway epithelial cells – Aspergillus fumigatus (strains Af293 or CEA10) were exposed to human cells for 6 or 16 hours.RNA-seq analysis was performed on both the host and fungal samples. Watkins et al. 2018. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.
  • Aspergillus niger
    • Aspergillus niger gene co-expression network – Co-expression network was generated from 155 RNA-Seq experiments. Schäpe et al. 2023.
  • Botrytis cinerea
    • Transcriptome profiling data of Botrytis cinerea infection on whole tomato plant – Transcriptome at 5 infection stages from B. cinerea infection sites. Srivastava et al. 2020.
  • Neurospora crassa
    • Transcriptome under standard minimal and iron strarvation conditions – Neurospora crassa grown under normal and iron starvation conditions. Bayram et al. 2018.
  • Candida albicans
    • RNA-seq of C. ablicans from cecum of monocolonized WT and Rag1-/- mice – Transcriptome of Candida albicans in response to antibody binding in the mouse (C57Bl/6) cecum. Ost et al. 2021. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.
  • Pyricularia oryzae
    • RNA-seq of mycelial cultures of Dhox7 and Dmst12 – Mycelial cultures of Dhox7 and Dmst12. Tang et al. 2023.

Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis

  • Candida auris
    • First five Candida auris isolates in Austria – Four isolates were characterized as South Asian clade I and one isolate as African clade III. Spettel et al. 2023.

Phenotype data

  • Candida spp.
    • Manually curated phenotypes from Candida Genome Database now appear on FungiDB gene records pages for Candida albicans SC5314, Candida auris B8441, Candida dubliniensis CD36, Candida parapsilosis CDC317, and Nakaseomyces glabratus CBS138.

Link Outs

  • Link outs to JGI MycoCosm were integrated for Aspergillus campestris IBT 28561, Aspergillus steynii IBT 23096, and Pleurotus ostreatus PC15.

New features in {{project}} 66

  • New options for downloading gene features are now availalbe from any gene record page. Click the Download Gene link at the top of the record page to use the new BED and FASTA download options.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
