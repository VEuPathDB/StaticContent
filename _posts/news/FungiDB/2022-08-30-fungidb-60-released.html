Transcriptomics

  • Botrytis cinerea
    • Transciptomic analysis of B. cinerea exposed to a bacterial biocontrol agent (secretome) — Transcriptome of B. cinerea in response to exposure to Bacillus subtilis MBI 600, a new commercialized biopesticide. Samaras et al. 2021.
  • Candida albicans
    • Genome-wide transcriptional analysis of tomatidine-treated C. albicans cells — Candida albicans was subjected to treatment with tomatidine and samples were taken at 2 different time points (1h, 3h). Dorsaz et al. 2017.

    • RNA-seq analysis of FSS1-dependent genes in Fusarium graminearum Z-3639 — The wild-type, fss1 deletion, fss1 overexpression mutant strains were incubated in potato dextrose broth (PDB) with or without 1 M NaCl supplementation for an hour. Son et al. 201.
  • Neurospora crassa
    • Transcriptomes of strains grown on different carbon sources (mono-, di- and polysaccharides) — Transcriptional profiling of N. crassa on different carbon sources. Wu et al. 2020.
  • Cryptococcus neoformans
    • Cryptococcus neoformans var. grubii KN99: Mapping TSS with TSS-Seq — Positioning the 5′ ends of coding genes. Wallace et al. 2020.
    • Cryptococcus neoformans JEC21: Mapping TSS with TSS-Seq — Positioning the 5′ ends of coding genes. Wallace et al. 2020.
    • Cryptococcus neoformans var. grubii KN99: Mapping 3′ ends of coding genes with QuantSeq 3′mRNA-Seq (PAS-Seq) — Positioning the 3′ ends of coding genes. Wallace et al. 2020.
    • Cryptococcus neoformans JEC21: Mapping 3′ ends of coding genes with QuantSeq 3′mRNA-Seq (PAS-Seq) — Positioning the 3′ ends of coding genes. Wallace et al. 2020.

ChiP-Seq

  • RNAP II ChIP-Seq for Aspergillus nidulans — ChIP-seq was performed on WT conidia to map the genome-wide occupancy of elongating RNA polymerase II (RNAP II). Wang et al. 2021.
  • Mapping histone modifications in Aspergillus nidulans spores — Mapping histone modifications in Aspergillus nidulans spores by ChIPseq (H3K36me3, H3K4me3, H3AC, H3) Wang et al. 2021.

New features in {{project}} 59

{{project}} site search accesses Apollo data

  • Gene annotation updates entered in {{project}} Apollo are now indexed and available to the site search. Genes with Apollo annotations that contain your search term will be available in site search results under >Genes >Filter gene fields.

Gene page navigation improvements

  • The RNA-Seq Transcription Summary graph now links directly to the Transcript Expression table. Click a data set title in the RNA-Seq Transcription Summary graph to navigate directly to the details for the same data set in the Transcript Expression table.
  • The gene page content navigation tool now contains links to every table or attribute on the gene page. Open each numbered data section to reveal links to all data within the section. Or use the 'expand all' link at the top of the content tool to view all links.

AlphaFold data available

  • Protein structure predictions from AlphaFold are now available in {{project}}. The AlphaFold database (Version 3) was downloaded and structures were associated to {{project}} genes via the UniProt ID. Jumper et al. 2021 and Varadi et al. 2022
  • The AlphaFold search returns all genes with AlphaFold predictions in {{project}}.
  • Gene pages include a table of AlphaFold data and the AlphaFold 3D viewer for dynamic visualization of the predicted structure.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
