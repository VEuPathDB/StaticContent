Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis:

  • Aspergillus fumigatus
    • SNP calls on WGS of Aspergillus fumigatus clinical, environmental, AsTEC and CDC study strains – The strain collection includes strains from CF patients followed over multiple years of infection (AF100 and AF106); environmental strains; AsTEC and CDC study strains. Ross et al. 2021.
    • SNP calls on Aspergillus fumigatus environmental isolates from Southeast USA – Environmental isolates from agricultural settings in Southeast USA. Kang et al. 2022.
  • Botrytis cinerea
    • SNP calls on WGS of Botrytis cinerea isolates – SNP calls on WGS of field isolates of Botrytis cinerea, largely collected from a local set of plant species. Atwell et al. 2018.
  • Candida auris
    • SNP calls on WGS of Candida auris identified in the Netherlands – Clinical strains collected from patients treated in the Netherlands. Vogelzang et al. 2019.
  • Pyricularia oryzae
    • SNP calls on WGS of Pyciularia oryzae isolates from different hosts – Isolates collected from the following hosts: Oryza sativa, Setaria italica, and Eleusine indica. Win et al. 2019.
    • SNP calls on WGS of Pyciularia oryzae isolates from Zambia – 13 Magnaporthe oryzae (syn. Pyricularia oryzae) isolates collected in Zambia from 2018 to 2020 during outbreaks of wheat blast. Tha Sainsbury Laboratory.

Transcriptomics

  • Aspergillus fumigatus
    • Transcriptomics analysis of Aspergillus fumigatus co-cultivated with Pseudomonas aeruginosa — Gene expression was determined at 45, 90 and 180 minutes after co-incubation. Vito Valiante 2018.
  • Candida albicans
    • Global translational landscape of the Candida albicans morphological transition — Candida albicans grown under 2 conditions (YEPD + serum at 37°C (filament-inducing) and YEPD at 30°C (non filament-inducing)). Mundodi et al. 2020.
    • Global transcriptional response to various environmental stresses, alone and in combinations — Transcriptome of C. albicans in response to exposure to H2O2, NaCL, and H2O2 & NaCl. Brown et al. 2015.
  • Cryptococcus neoformans
    • Stranded mRNA-Seq for delineation of transcript ends in C. neoformans JEC21 — Stranded RNA-Seq from exponentially and stationary phases of growth. Wallace et al. 2020.
  • Fusarium mangiferae
    • In planta expression profiles of Fusarium magniferae — Transcriptional changes during maize root infection. Root samples were taken 4, 10, and 20 dpi for RNA isolation. Niehaus et al. 2015.
  • Neurospora crassa
    • Transcriptome of N. crassa strains grown on different carbon sources (complex polysaccharides) — Transcriptional profiling dataset series determining how fungi sense simple to complex carbohydrates Wu et al. 2020.
  • Puccinia striiformis f. sp. tritici
    • RNA sequencing of stripe rust infected wheat leaves and isolated haustoria — Total RNA was extracted from a pool of stripe rust infected wheat leaves and from two biological replicates of haustoria isolates. Cantu et al. 2013.
  • Zymoseptoria tritici
    • Small RNA profiling of the infection cycle of Zymoseptoria tritici on wheat — Samples were collected at 4, 9, 13, and 21 days post infection. Kettles et al. 2019.

New features in {{project}} 59

{{project}} site search accesses Apollo data

  • Gene annotation updates entered in {{project}} Apollo are now indexed and available to the site search. Genes with Apollo annotations that contain your search term will be available in site search results under >Genes >Filter gene fields.

Gene page navigation improvements

  • The RNA-Seq Transcription Summary graph now links directly to the Transcript Expression table. Click a data set title in the RNA-Seq Transcription Summary graph to navigate directly to the details for the same data set in the Transcript Expression table.
  • The gene page content navigation tool now contains links to every table or attribute on the gene page. Open each numbered data section to reveal links to all data within the section. Or use the 'expand all' link at the top of the content tool to view all links.

AlphaFold data available

  • Protein structure predictions from AlphaFold are now available in {{project}}. The AlphaFold database (Version 3) was downloaded and structures were associated to {{project}} genes via the UniProt ID. Jumper et al. 2021 and Varadi et al. 2022
  • The AlphaFold search returns all genes with AlphaFold predictions in {{project}}.
  • Gene pages include a table of AlphaFold data and the AlphaFold 3D viewer for dynamic visualization of the predicted structure.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
