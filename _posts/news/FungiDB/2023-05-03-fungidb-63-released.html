  • Cryptococcus neoformans strain:VNII – Sephton-Clark et al. 2023.
  • Fusarium solani FSSC 5 MPI-SDFR-AT-0091 – Mesny et al. 2021.
  • Kluyveromyces marxianus DMKU3-1042 – Lertwattanasakul et al. 2015.
  • Monilinia fructicola CPMC6 – Vilanova et al. 2021

  • Pneumocystis carinii B80 – Ma et al. 2013.
  • Rickenella mellea Ricmel1 – Krizsán et al. 2019.

Transcriptomics

  • Candida dubliniensis
    • Transcriptional responses to macrophage phagocytosis — The transcriptional responses to phagocytosis was analysed by incubating fungal cells in mammalian medium in the presence or absence of primary macrophages for 1 hour, followed by RNA extraction and sequencing. Poutain al. 2021. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.
  • Clavispora lusitaniae
    • Transcriptional responses to macrophage phagocytosis — The transcriptional responses to phagocytosis was analysed by incubating fungal cells in mammalian medium in the presence or absence of primary macrophages for 1 hour, followed by RNA extraction and sequencing. Poutain al. 2021. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.
  • Lodderomyces elongisporus
    • Transcriptional responses to macrophage phagocytosis — The transcriptional responses to phagocytosis was analysed by incubating fungal cells in mammalian medium in the presence or absence of primary macrophages for 1 hour, followed by RNA extraction and sequencing. Poutain al. 2021. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.
  • Histoplasma capsulatum
    • Comparative transcriptomes of yeast and hyphal forms in Histoplasma capsulatum G184AR — Analysis of yeast and hyphal transcript expression patterns in Histoplasma capsulatum grown at 37°C and room temperature (RT), respectively. Gilmore al. 2015.
  • Neurospora crassa
    • Gene expression during conidia germination in Neurospora crassa on different media — mRNA were sampled and compared from four time points across conidia germination in Neurospora crassa on different media. Wang al. 2019.
  • Pneumocystis carinii
    • Pneumocystis carinii RNA-Seq — RNA-Seq of three samples each of Peumocystis carinii were isolated from infected rat hosts. Ma al. 2016.

Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis

  • Aspergillus fumigatus
    • SNP calls on WGS of Aspergillus fumigatus isolates from the UK and Ireland — Population genomics confirms acquisition of drug-resistant Aspergillus fumigatus infection by humans from the environment. Rhodes al. 2022.
  • Candida auris
    • SNP calls on WGS of clinical isolates passaged for multiple generations — In vitro evolution of Candida auris clinical isolates in 1ug/ml fluconazole for 30 generations. Burrack et al. 2022.
  • Cryptococcus gattii
    • SNP calls on WGS of clinical strains from South African laboratory-based surveillance, 2005-2013 — Whole genome sequencing (WGS) was performed on 98 molecular type VGIV isolates. Naicker et al. 2022.
  • Sporothrix brasiliensis
    • SNP calls on WGS of Sporothrix brasiliensis isolates — Natural variation of Sporothrix brasiliensis in different epidemic areas of the Brazilian territory. Filho et al. 2020.
  • Sporothrix schenckii
    • SNP calls on WGS of Sporothrix schenckii isolates — Natural variation of Sporothrix schenckii in different epidemic areas of the Brazilian territory. Filho et al. 2020.

New features in {{project}} 63

  • Download Data Files BETA – {{project}} has a new tool for downloading genome scale files such as genome.fasta or GFF files. Similar to other {{project}} tools, all data and files are available by default. To limit the result, choose a value (right) for one or more categories (left). From the filtered list, download a single file by clicking the file name, or multiple files using the check boxes and 'Dowload selected files' button. Download Beta tutorial GIF.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
