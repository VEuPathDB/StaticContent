New data in FungiDB 66

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Chaetothyriales sp. CBS 132003 – Westerdijk Fungal Biodiversity Institute.
  • Leptosphaeria maculans JN3 – Rouxel et al. 2012.

Genome replacements

  • Coccidioides immitis H538.4 – Neafsey et al. 2010.
  • Malassezia globosa CBS 7966 – Xu et al. 2007.
  • Rhizophagus irregularis DAOM 197198 – Yildirir et al. 2021.

Transcriptomics

  • Aspergillus fumigatus
    • Transcriptomics of Aspergillus fumigatus strains upon exposure to human airway epithelial cells – Aspergillus fumigatus (strains Af293 or CEA10) for 6 or 16 hours and then subject to RNA-seq analysis of both the host and fungal transriptomes. Watkins et al. 2018. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.
  • Botrytis cinerea
    • Transcriptome profiling data of Botrytis cinerea infection on whole tomato plant – Transcriptome at 5 infection stages from B. cinerea infection sites. Srivastava et al. 2020.
  • Neurospora crassa
    • Transcriptome under standard minimal and iron strarvation conditions – Neurospora crassa grown under normal and iron starvation conditions. Bayram et al. 2018.
  • Candda albicans
    • RNA-seq of C. ablicans from cecum of monocolonized WT and Rag1-/- mice – Transcriptome of Candida albicans in response to antibody binding in the mouse (C57Bl/6) cecum. Ost et al. 2021. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.

Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis

  • Aspergillus fumigatus
    • SNP calls on WGS of serial isolates from clinical settings – Whole-genome comparison of Aspergillus fumigatus strains serially isolated from patients with aspergillosis. Hagiwara et al. 2014.
  • Candida auris
    • SNP calls on WGS of Candida auris in Nevada – Sequencing was performed by Nevada State Public Health Laboratory submission group.
    • SNP calls on WGS of Candida auris involved in an hospital outbreak (Italy) – All isolates were resistant to amphotericin B, voriconazole, and fluconazole at high-level, owing to mutations in ERG11(K143R) and TACB1(A640V). Pilato et al. 2021
    • SNP calls on WGS of Candida auris in the United States (CDC) – Sequencing was performed by CDC National Center for Emerging and Zoonotic Infectious Diseases - Mycotic Diseases Branch.
  • Cryptococcus neoformans
    • SNP calls on WGS of clinical Cryptococcus neoformans isolates from the ACTA clinical trial – Cryptococcus neoformans recovered from the cerebral spinal fluid (CSF) of an immunocompromised patient. Sephton-Clark et al. 2022
  • Pyricularia oryzae
    • SNP calls on WGS of GE10A2 and GE12B isolates – Two Setaria spp.-infecting isolates with contrasting colony morphologies.

New features in {{project}} 66

  • The {{project}} webinars page has a new look. 'Upcoming' webinars announce the next few webinars. 'Releases' contains the recordings for our previous release webinars. 'On-Demand Training' provides embedded videos for special topic webinars. All webinar recordings are also available from the VEuPathDB YouTube Channel.
  • OrthoMCL DB offers a new preconfigured download table called Phyletic Distribution Clade Abundance that offers the OMCL group distribution of sequences in Alveolata, Archaea, Amoeba, Bacteria, Fungi, Euglenozoa, Metazoa, Viridiplantae, and other Eukaryotes. OMCL Download Tutorial
