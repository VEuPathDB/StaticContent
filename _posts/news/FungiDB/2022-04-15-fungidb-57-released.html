Transcriptomics

  • Aspergillus fumigatus
    • Determining Aspergillus fumigatus transcription factor expression and function during invasion of the mammalian lung — RNA-seq analysis was performed on wild-type and an ACE1 deletion mutant. Liu et al. 2021.
  • Candida albicans
    • Transcriptomes of clinical isolate strains and mutant derivatives — Transcriptome of the fungi in infected maize roots growing in CM agar. Huang et al. 2019.
    • Transcriptomic analysis of wild type and GCN5-deficient Candida albicans cells prior to and after treatment with caspofungin — Investigation of the virulence factors in C. albicans Shivarathri et al. 2019.
    • CalCEN: Candida albicans co-expression Network v1.0.0 — Raw reads from 853 RNA-seq runs of 18 large-scale Candida albicans studies were collected from NCBI and re-mapped to 6226 open reading frames of the SC5314 strain. The CalCEN co-expression network was then estimated by the pairwise spearman rank-correlation of FPKM expression values across all runs. Teresa O'Meara and Matthew O'Meara, 2021.
  • Candida auris
    • Experimental evolution of fluconazole resistance in Candida auris — Transcriptomic profiles of drug resistant and sensitive strains of C. auris. Bing et al. 2020.

  • Candida lusitaniae
    • Transcriptional profiling of strains with different levels of Mrr1 activity in Candida lusitaniae — Expression profiling by high throughput sequencing. Demers et al. 2021.
  • Coccidioides posadasii
    • Transcriptomics of environmental (mycelia) and parasitic (spherule 96 hr) stages of the Δcts2/Δard1/Δcts3 strain — An attenuated mutant strain Δcts2/Δard1/Δcts3 in which chitinase genes 2 and 3 were deleted was previously created for vaccine development and profiled by RNA-Seq at two conditions. New features in {{project}} 56

BLAST Tool Improvements

  • The BLAST (multi-query capable) tool, known previously as BLASTbeta, can perform queries with one or more FASTA-formatted sequences and offers many download types. The tool uses NCBI BLAST to find genes, genomic sequences, ESTs, or PopSet Sequences that have BLAST similarity to your input sequence. BLAST (multi-query capable) is available from the tools menu.
  • A new download option, 'This table (csv)', for the results of multi-sequence queries (combined result) retrieves the {{project}} result table for the results of multi-sequence queries (combined result).
  • View tutorial GIF.

Gene page sequence highlighting

  • Genomic and mRNA sequences on gene pages have a new color scheme. Introns are highlighted in yellow and UTR sequence are highlighted in pink. Previously they were gray and blue, respectively. Example


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
