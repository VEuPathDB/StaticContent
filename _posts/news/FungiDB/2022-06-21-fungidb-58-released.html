ChiP-Seq

  • RNAP II ChIP-Seq data for Talaromyces marneffei— ChIP-seq was performed on WT conidia to map the genome-wide occupancy of elongating RNA polymerase II (RNAP II). Wang et al. 2021.

Function prediction

  • CalCENv1 co-expression network UMAP clusters — CalCENv1 co-expression network was projected to two dimensions using UMAP and 18 clusters were identified and annotated through gene set enrichment analysis. O'Meara and O'Meara, 2021.

Phenotype data

  • Phenotypes from PHI-base database — PHI-base is a web-accessible database that catalogues experimentally verified pathogenicity, virulence and effector genes from fungal, oomycete and bacterial pathogens, which infect animal, plant, fungal and insect hosts. The pathogen-host interactions (PHI) phenotypes and pathogen-specific phenotypes from PHI-base were integrated for 61 different genomes available in FungiDB in this release. This dataset is current with PHI-base version 4.12. We have integrated 5,381 phenotypic annotations mapping to 3,140 genes in total. The original data file from PHI-base can be found on PHI-base GitHub site.

New features in {{project}} 57

New Searches

  • The new Unannotated Intron Junctions search enables users to identify genes that contain, or are flanked by, unannotated high confidence intron junction-spanning reads from RNA-seq data. These genes may be incompletely or inaccurately annotated due to missing introns/exons and/or alternative splice variants. The search is categorized under Search for Genes, Function Prediction. See our PDF Tutorial or our Video Tutorial for more information.
  • The new Dataset Gene List search enables users to search for genes in co-expression clusters associated with O'Meara et al 2021.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
