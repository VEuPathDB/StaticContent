New data in FungiDB 62

Transcriptomics

  • Botrytis cinerea
    • RNA-seq of Botrytis cinerea isolates from Spain and Italy — Examination of mRNA patterns of 4 transcription factor mutants of Aspergillus fumigatus and its parental strains in presence of 2 concentrations of Congo Red. Liu et al. 2021.
  • Candida albicans
    • Candida transcriptomes during oropharyngeal candidiasis infection in mouses — Transcriptional responses to macrophage phagocytosis by Candida albicans. Pountain et al. 2021. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.
  • Cordyceps militaris
    • RNA-Seq data of Cordyceps militaris cultivated on the germinated soybeans — Transcriptional responses to macrophage phagocytosis by Candida parapsilosis. Pountain et al. 2021. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.

    Transcriptomics

    • Aspergillus fumigatus
      • SNP calls on WGS on avian isolates of Aspergillus fumigatus — Examination of mRNA patterns of 4 transcription factor mutants of Aspergillus fumigatus and its parental strains in presence of 2 concentrations of Congo Red. Liu et al. 2021.
    • Candida auris
      • SNP calls on WGS of Candida auris on apples — Transcriptional responses to macrophage phagocytosis by Candida albicans. Pountain et al. 2021. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.
      • SNP calls on WGS of Candida auris identified in Canada, 2012-2019 — Transcriptional responses to macrophage phagocytosis by Candida albicans. Pountain et al. 2021. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.

      • SNP calls on WGS of C. gattii from North American Pacific Northwest — Transcriptional responses to macrophage phagocytosis by Candida parapsilosis. Pountain et al. 2021. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.
    • Neurospora discreta
      • SNP calls on WGS of Neurospora discreta crosses — Transcriptional responses to macrophage phagocytosis by Candida parapsilosis. Pountain et al. 2021. This FungiDB data set has associated data sets.
