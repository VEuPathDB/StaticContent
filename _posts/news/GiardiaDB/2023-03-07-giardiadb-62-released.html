New data in {{project}} 62

Genome Update

  • Spironucleus salmonicida ATCC50377 – This genome was updated to the current INSDC version which is resequenced, reassembled and reannotated. GCA_000497125.2.

RNA Sequence

  • Encysting Giardia during infection in human IEC – RNA-seq data from encysting Giardia cells (7hr encysting) during an infection in human Caco-2 cells. Rojas et al. 2022
  • Giardia Encystation Time Course – Gene expression changes during encystation of Giardia intenstinalis, measured every 3.5 hr and analyzed by RNA sequence. Rojas-Lopez et al. 2021
  • Giardia Infection in human IEC – Gene expression changes during Giardia intenstinalis infection of human intestinal epithelial cells (differentiated Caco-2 cells). Three biological replicates of control and 1.5hr, 3hr, and 4.5hr time points measured were analyzed by RNA sequencing. Rojas et al. 2022
  • Metronidazole resistant cell line transcriptomes – Transcriptomes of two in vitro selected, metronidazole resistant parasite lines, as well as one revertant and the wild type cell line. Krakovka et al. 2022

New Alignments

The following data sets have been aligned to Giardia Assemblage A isolate WB 2019.
  • Assemblage A intestine foci transcriptome (gassAWB2019 aligned) – Transcriptome from Giardia foci in murine intestine. Pham et al. 2017
  • Time-dependent transcriptional changes in axenic trophozoites (gassAWB2019 aligned) – Transcriptome at 48, 60, and 98hr cultures. Ansell et al. 2015
  • Trophozoite transcriptome (gassAWB2019 aligned) – Trophozoite transcriptome of Giardia lamblia Assemblage A isolate WB (ATCC 50803) determined by RNA-Seq Tolba et al. 2013
  • Trophozoite Transcriptome of Giardia assemblage A strain AS175 (gassAWB2019 aligned) – Trophozoite transcriptome of G. Assemblage A strain AS175 at passage 4 and 33 Franzén et al. 2013
