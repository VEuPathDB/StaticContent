New data in {{project}} 64

Quantitative Proteomics

  • Encystation proteome (quantitative analysis) – Complete observation of encystation at the proteomic level up to mature cyst. Samples include trophozoite, low-bile encystation, 16hr encysting, cyst maturation Balan et al. 2020.
  • Metronidazole resistant cell line proteomes – Proteomic analysis of two in vitro selected, metronidazole resistant parasite lines, (M1 and M2) as well as one revertant (M1NR) and the wild type cell line (WB). Krakova et al. 2022.

Proteomics (mapped peptides)

  • Metronidazole resistant cell line proteome (mapped peptides) – Proteomic analysis of two in vitro selected, metronidazole resistant parasite lines, (M1 and M2) as well as one revertant (M1NR) and the wild type cell line (WB). Krakova et al. 2022.

New features in {{project}} 64

  • Download Data Files BETA – {{project}} has a new tool for downloading genome scale files such as genome.fasta or GFF files. Similar to other {{project}} tools, all data and files are available by default. To limit the result, choose a value (right) for one or more categories (left). From the filtered list, download a single file by clicking the file name, or multiple files using the check boxes and 'Dowload selected files' button. View Download Beta tutorial GIF.
