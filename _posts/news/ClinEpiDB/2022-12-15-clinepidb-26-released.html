We are pleased to announce the release of ClinEpiDB 26!

New Data

  • The Study of Illness in Preterm (SIP) cohort study is a longitudinal cohort study of preterm birth conducted in 3 different regions of Ethiopia. Preterm infants less than 7 days old were enrolled and followed over the first 28 days of life or until death. The primary objective of this study was to determine the most common causes of illness and mortality in preterm infants admitted to hospitals in Ethiopia.

  • You can make a Mosaic plot, RxC table on ClinEpiDB. A mosaic plot is a special type of stacked bar chart which provides a useful way to explore the relationship between two categorical variables. The size of each tile is proportional to the number of observations in that category. You can view the underlying contingency table and chi square statistics on the Table and Statistics tabs, respectively.
