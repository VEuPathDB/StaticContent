We are pleased to announce the release of ClinEpiDB 20 on beta.clinepidb.org!

New Features

  • Notes tab - If you open a study, there's a new Notes tab where you can jot down your hypothesis, reminders about specific variables, plans to subset the data, thoughts about what graphs to create, etc. Notes will be automatically saved as part of your analysis.
  • Facet plots - All visualization tools (histograms, bar plots, scatter plots, etc.) now give you the option to facet your plot by another variable.

New Data

  • The Early Life Interventions for Childhood Growth and Development in Tanzania (ELICIT) study is the first new study to be released on the new platform! This randomized controlled trial evaluated the efficacy of antimicrobials (azithromycin and nitazoxanide) and nicotinamide in increasing length-for-age z-score in a 2x2 factorial trial in Tanzania.
  • Six studies have been added to beta.clinepidb.org. These include enteric disease studies GEMS1 HUAS/HUAS Lite Survey and PROVIDE Randomized Controlled Trial, malaria studies PRISM ICEMR Cohort and LLINEUP Cluster Randomized Trial, and schistosomiasis studies SCORE S. mansoni Kenya and Tanzania Cohort and SCORE Seasonal S. haematobium Cluster Randomized Trial.

Workshops
