Pseudogene Classification

In release 51 (16 March 2021) pseudogenes with an internal stop codon automatically included predicted 3'UTRs. The UTR prediction was due to an incorrect pipeline which has now been rectified. UTRs have now been removed and all pseudogenes are correctly displayed without CDS and UTR. The affected pseudogenes were shown in VEuPathDB with the Biotype classification pseudogene_with_CDS. This has now been changed to the correct biotype pseudogene. List of affected genes.

New data in {{project}} 60

Genome Sequence

  • Enterocytozoon hepatopenaei EHP-ID16 – isolated from Penaeus vannamei cultured at Indonesia. submitted by Ji Hyung Kim GCA_003709115.1

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Hamiltosporidium magnivora BE-OM-2 – isolated from cultured Daphnia magna in Belgium. Haag et al. 2020
  • Hamiltosporidium magnivora IL-BN-2 – isolated from cultured Daphnia magna in Israel. Haag et al. 2020
  • Hamiltosporidium tvaerminnensis IL-G-3 – isolated from cultured Daphnia magna from Israel: Gaash Pond. Haag et al. 2020
  • Hamiltosporidium tvaerminnensis FI-OER-3-3 – isolated from cultured Daphnia magna from Finland: Oeren Tvarminne Achipelago. Haag et al. 2020
  • Mitosporidium daphniae UGP3 – Updated to the new repository version to incorporate Feb 2020 changes including the supression of two contigs (contaminants: JMKJ01000282 and JMKJ01000400), and a new Assembly Name was (changed from UGP1.0 to UGP1.1). Haag et al. 2020
  • Microsporidium sp. FI-F-10 – isolated from the infected the gut epithelium cells of its crustacean host Daphnia longispina sampled in Finland: skerry island on the Baltic Sea near Hanko-Tvarminne. Haag et al. 2020
  • Nematocida ironsii ERTm5 – an intestinal-specific strain isolated from a wild-caught C. briggsae in Hawaii. This organism name was changed from Nematocida sp. ERTm5 to Nematocida ironsii ERTm5 as documented in Reinke et al. 2017. The NCBI record (PRJNA312551) reflects the original name. Balla et al. 2019
  • Nosema apis BRL 01 – a microsporidia infecting the honey bee. Chen et al. 2013

Structure Predictions

  • AlphaFold Predictions – are updated to AlphaFold Release 4 (July 2022) which offers predictions for significantly more proteins than Release 3. Use {{project}}'s Genes with AlphaFold Predictions search to explore genes with AlphaFold predictions or visit the Structure Analysis section of your favorite gene page to view data relevant to you.

New features in {{project}} 60

Associated Data Sets

  • Data sets that come from the same publication are now linked. The new 'Associated Data Sets' section on any data set record page will link to other data from the same publicaion within {{project}} as well as data from organisms supported in different VEuPathDB sites. View tutorial GIF.
