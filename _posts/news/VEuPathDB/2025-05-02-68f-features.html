VEuPathDB Sustainability

SUBSCRIPTIONS

VEuPathDB is freely accessible to anyone, but maintaining and updating database resources is not free. The changing funding landscape necessitates a mandatory subscription service, even if the applicable cost for your lab is $0 (subsidized). Fees for “website access and data sharing & dissemination” are considered an allowable expense by most funding agencies. Visit our subscriptions page today to learn more, and generate an invoice for your individual grant, laboratory, or organization … thereby doing your part to keep VEuPathDB resources open for all. Your support is crucial!

DONATIONS

New Release

ORTHOMCL-7

The new OrthoMCL release is now live at OrthoMCL.org, allowing you to identify orthologs across species, and map your own proteins. Notable updates: proteomes have been refreshed with current UniProt annotation. Orthogroups are now clustered using the OrthoFinder algorithm. Web pages include a customizable phylogenetic tree and highlight similar groups, helping identify protein superfamilies. OrthoMCL-6.21 remains accessible as a Legacy site.

New Data

GENOME-WIDE CRISPR SCREENS

ToxoDB now includes data from functional profiling of the entire Toxoplasma gondii genome during cell culture and murine infection using a CRISPR-based screen (Guiliano et al 2024). See the dataset here and explore the data here. Datasets are available under Searches > Genes > Phenotype > Phenotype Evidence, and data for individual genes is represented on Gene Pages under Phenotype Graphs.

Community Updates

GP60 RESOURCE PAGE

Cryptosporidium molecular epidemiology relies on subtyping of the gp60 gene. A recent publication by Robinson et al compiles rules for gp60 subtyping nomenclature, and CryptoDB now hosts a gp60 Resource Page, collating resources and promoting standardization of gp60 subtyping. This page links to the latest version of the gp60 subtyping table, and a contact form to notify curators of novel designations and updates.
