VEuPathDB Sustainability

UPDATE ON FUNDING STATUS

Based on User Survey results suggesting widespread willingness of PIs to contribute their fair share of VEuPathDB expenses, we released a voluntary subscription service in March 2025. Receipts to date total ~$1M, with additional invoices arriving daily. In combination with grants and other financial commitments, this covers about half of our projected $4M budget for 2025-26, enabling us to avoid issuing staff termination notices in June. We are also making steady progress on securing supplementary funding from philanthropic organizations to bridge the remaining gap, but still need additional user subscriptions. We thank everyone who has contributed to date, and encourage those who have not yet done so to subscribe or donate as soon as possible. Please also be sure to include support for VEuPathDB as part of your data-sharing budget in new grants (see FAQ). Your continued support is essential for keeping VEuPathDB resources freely available to all!
