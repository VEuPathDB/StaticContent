We are pleased to announce a joint VEuPathDB and BV-BRC open community webinar series focusing on ticks and the bacterial, viral or eukaryotic pathogens they transmit. Eight webinars will be held on a bi-weekly basis starting on March 8th. Each webinar will focus one specific data, tools and functionality provided by these NIAID-funded bioinformatic resource centers.
