New Feature

AI-POWERED SUMMARIES OF EXPRESSION DATA

Big News! We're now offering AI-powered summaries of expression data on gene pages! Our new AI tool analyzes and summarizes transcriptomics data, providing you with easy-to-read insights and relevance scores.

How it works: This experimental feature uses Al to help interpret the gene's expression data shown in the "Transcript Expression" section further down the gene page. First, the Al analyzes each individual experiment using its numeric data and metadata to generate a one-sentence summary and an estimated biological relevance score. Then, those per-experiment summaries are grouped into topics to highlight common expression patterns across experiments. The AI Expression Summary feature is available for genes with >= 5 transcriptomics datasets.

Learn more by watching this 2-minute video on YouTube.

See examples of AI expression summaries on AmoebaDB, CryptoDB, FungiDB, PlasmoDB, ToxoDB, TriTrypDB, VectorBase.

Try it for yourself: Visit the gene page for your favorite gene. Search for “AI Expression Summary” in the left sidebar. Click the “Start AI Summary” button.

Give us your feedback: Tell us whether the AI summary was helpful by giving it a thumbs-up or thumbs-down and filling in an optional survey.

Disclaimer: This is an experimental feature. Please be aware that summaries provided by AI are designed to aid in interpreting gene expression data but may occasionally misrepresent the underlying results.
