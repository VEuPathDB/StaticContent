We are pleased to announce the launch of the BBR24 grant, a new FungiDB/VEuPathDB & Ensembl partnership, supported by the Bioinformatics and Biological Resources Fund. This is a three-year initiative to expand resources for fungal and oomycete plant pathogens.

New Features

VEuPathDB ACCOUNT FEATURES

We have implemented two new features on VEuPathDB accounts.

Delete my account: Users now have a straightforward way to delete their VEuPathDB accounts and anonymize associated data. This aligns with prevailing data privacy standards. You can find this feature in the Profile section of your account- please exercise caution as deleting your account is a permanent action!

Classroom subscriptions: Users who sign up for a VEuPathDB account for temporary use, such as attending a course, class, or workshop can now take advantage of a free temporary classroom subscription. The Profile section of your account has a field for “Group type”. Choosing the option “Workshop or Class (temporary student group)” and clicking save will initiate this special complementary classroom subscription. VEuPathDB is proud to support use of our resources to further classroom learning for the next generation of bioinformaticians!
