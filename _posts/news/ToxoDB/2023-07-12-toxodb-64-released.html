New data in {{project}} 64

RNA Sequence

Single-cell RNA Sequence

  • A single-parasite transcriptional atlas of Toxoplasma gondii PRU – Single-cell RNA-sequencing (scRNA-seq) on > 5,400 Toxoplasma in both tachyzoite and bradyzoite stages. Zue et al. 2020
  • A single-parasite transcriptional atlas of Toxoplasma gondii RH – Single-cell RNA-sequencing (scRNA-seq) on > 5,400 Toxoplasma in both tachyzoite and bradyzoite stages. Zue et al. 2020

Proteomics

New features in {{project}} 64

  • VEuPathDB Galaxy now offers a preconfigured ChIP Seq workflow. This workflow processes ChIP Seq FASTQ files by aligning to your chosen reference genome with BWA then calling peaks using MACS2. Peaks calculated from the input FASTQ files are converted to bigWig format which can be exported from Galaxy to your VEuPathDB account.
  • Strategy Improvement – A new option in the gene result table provides a view of search specific data columns for your preferred subset of genes. Click the 'Show only the Genes in my basket' option from any gene result to filter the result based on the genes in your basket. This can be used in combination with the 'Send to Basket (replace)' option to view search specific data columns for multi-step strategy results. See Filter by Basket tutorial.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary.
