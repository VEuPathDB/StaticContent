New data in {{project}} 62

Genome updates:

  • Hammondia hammondi H.H.34 – The assembly was updated to the current INSDC version. GCA_000258005.2.

RNA Sequence

  • Tanscriptome from asexual to sexual in vitro development of Cystoisospora suis – transcriptional profiles at three different time points of development corresponding to asexual, immature and mature sexual stages, determined in vitro via RNA Seq. Cruz-Bustos et al. 2022.
  • Unsporulated and sporulated T. gondii – Next-Generation RNA-Sequencing was used to analyse the transcriptome of oocysts in the process of sporulation and compared it to unsporulated and sporulated oocysts. PRJNA850353.
