New data in {{project}} 65

Bulk RNA Sequence

  • Stage-specific RNA-sequencing in Toxoplasma gondii – Stage-specific bulk RNA-Seq in Toxoplasma gondii in unstressed (24 h, tachyzoite) or stressed (48 h, bradyzoite) conditions. Originally loaded in ToxoDB 54, these data were reanalyzed in ToxoDB 65 to ensure the proper treatment of technical and biological replicates. Waldman et al. 2020

Single-cell RNA Sequence

  • A single-parasite transcriptional atlas of Toxoplasma gondii RH – Single-cell RNA-sequencing (scRNA-seq) on > 5,400 Toxoplasma in both tachyzoite and bradyzoite stages. Xue et al. 2020

Updated Functional Annotation

Functional annotations in ToxoDB have been updated for T. gondii ME49. For details please see this Summary of annotation updates which include products, GO terms, gene names, synonyms and PMID.

New features in {{project}} 65

  • VEuPathDB Galaxy now offers a preconfigured ChIP Seq workflow. This workflow processes ChIP Seq FASTQ files by aligning to your chosen reference genome with BWA then calling peaks using MACS2. Peaks calculated from the input FASTQ files are converted to bigWig format which can be exported from Galaxy to your VEuPathDB account.
  • Strategy Improvement – A new option in the gene result table provides a view of search specific data columns for your preferred subset of genes. Click the 'Show only the Genes in my basket' option from any gene result to filter the result based on the genes in your basket. This can be used in combination with the 'Send to Basket (replace)' option to view search specific data columns for multi-step strategy results. Filter by Basket tutorial.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary.
