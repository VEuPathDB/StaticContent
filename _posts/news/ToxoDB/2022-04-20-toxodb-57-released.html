New data in {{project}} 57

RNA Sequence

  • Eimeria tenella Houghton

    • Transcriptome of E. tenella from infected chicken caecal tissues – Transcriptome of Eimeria tenella from infected chicken caecal tissues. Chickens were infected with purified sporozoites and RNA-Seq was performed on samples collected 3, 4, and 10 days post-infection Sandholt et al. 2021. The host-aligned data analysis of this dual RNA seq dataset appears in HostDB: Chicken caecal tissue during E tenella infection.
    • Eimeria tenella transcriptome during infection in chicken macrophage-like cells – Transcriptome of Eimeria tenella during infection in macrophage-like cell line HD11 cultures infected in vitro. Triplicate cultures were harvested at 2, 4, 12, 24, 48 and 72 hr post-infection and mRNA was extracted and sequenced. Sandholt et al. 2021. The host-aligned data analysis of this dual RNA seq dataset appears in HostDB: Chicken macrophage-like cells (HD11) infected in vitro with Eimeria tenella infection.

  • Toxoplasma gondii ME49

    • RNA-Seq of MORC WT and MORC KD parasites – Microrchidia (MORC) protein transcriptome changes at different time points and FR235222 treatment. Farhat et al. 2020.
    • RNA-Seq of CPSF4 WT and CPSF4 KD parasites – RNA profiles were generated from HFF cells infected by RHDku80 CPSF4-mAID-HA T. gondii strain for untreated conditions, auxin induced KD at 7h, 24h, and 48h of CPSF4. Farhat et al. 2021.
    • T. gondii during spontaneous differentiation in primary rat brain cells. – Transcriptome changes during the parasite spontaneous differentiation after infection of primary rat brain cells. Triplicate RNA samples of infected primary CNS cell culture at 1d, 2d, 4d, 7d, and 14d post-infection. Farhat et al. 2021. The host-aligned data analysis of this dual RNA seq dataset appears in HostDB: Rat brain primary cells transcriptome during infection with T. gondii.

ChIP Sequence

  • ChIP-Seq in MORC WT and MORC KD parasites – Genome-wide MORC and HDAC3 occupancy profiles in Pruku80_MORC_mAID_HA_KD left untreated (UT) and treated with IAA (indole-3-acetic acid) for 30 hours. Farhat et al. 2020.

New features in {{project}} 57

BLAST Tool Improvements

  • The BLAST (multi-query capable) tool, known previously as BLASTbeta, can perform queries with one or more FASTA-formatted sequences and offers many download types. The tool uses NCBI BLAST to find genes, genomic sequences, ESTs, or PopSet Sequences that have BLAST similarity to your input sequence. BLAST (multi-query capable) is available from the tools menu.
  • A new download option, 'This table (csv)', for the results of multi-sequence queries (combined result) retrieves the {{project}} result table for the results of multi-sequence queries (combined result).
  • View tutorial GIF.

Gene page sequence highlighting

  • Genomic and mRNA sequences on gene pages have a new color scheme. Introns are highlighted in yellow and UTR sequence are highlighted in pink. Previously they were gray and blue, respectively. Example
