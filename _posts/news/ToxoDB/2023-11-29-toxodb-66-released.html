New data in {{project}} 66

Annotation Updates

  • Sarcocystis neurona SO SN1 – Pfam domain names were substituted for product descriptions for genes with assigned Pfam domains and uninformative product descriptions such as 'unspecified product'. 3122 genes were given new product descriptions.

Bulk RNA Sequence

  • Stage-specific RNA-sequencing in Toxoplasma gondii – Stage-specific bulk RNA-Seq in Toxoplasma gondii in unstressed (24 h, tachyzoite) or stressed (48 h, bradyzoite) conditions. Originally loaded in ToxoDB 54, these data were reanalyzed in ToxoDB 66 to ensure the proper treatment of technical and biological replicates. Waldman et al. 2020

Single-cell RNA Sequence

  • A single-parasite transcriptional atlas of Toxoplasma gondii RH – Single-cell RNA-sequencing (scRNA-seq) on > 5,400 Toxoplasma in both tachyzoite and bradyzoite stages. Xue et al. 2020

Updated Functional Annotation

Functional annotations in ToxoDB have been updated for T. gondii ME49. For details please see this Summary of annotation updates which include products, GO terms, gene names, synonyms and PMID.

New features in {{project}} 66

  • The {{project}} webinars page has a new look. 'Upcoming' webinars announce the next few webinars. 'Releases' contains the recordings for our previous release webinars. 'On-Demand Training' provides embedded videos for special topic webinars. All webinar recordings are also available from the VEuPathDB YouTube Channel.
  • OrthoMCL DB offers a new preconfigured download table called Phyletic Distribution Clade Abundance that offers the OMCL group distribution of sequences in Alveolata, Archaea, Amoeba, Bacteria, Fungi, Euglenozoa, Metazoa, Viridiplantae, and other Eukaryotes. OMCL Download Tutorial
