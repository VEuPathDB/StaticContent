Structural Gene Model Updates

Toxoplasma gondii ME49 – Significant updates were made to structural gene models based on community input, scientific publication, species comparisons, conserved domains and more. Following a recorded community meeting, changes were made by community members and VEuPathDB curators via ToxoDB Apollo. A summary and detailed list of all changes is available in the Structural_Annotation_TgondiiME49 spreadsheet.

The integration process provides automatically generated systematic IDs for new genes, merged and split genes. For genes that were modified (change of exon/intron boundary, addition of UTRs or change of UTR coordinates), transcript IDs have been changed. All IDs are mapped between versions – the Names, Previous Identifiers, and Aliases table on gene pages contain previous IDs and searching for a previous ID will return the records associated with the new ID. A 'TgME49 transcripts from Release 65' JBrowse data track was created for comparison. 5 newly annotated pseudogenes that still overlap with the previous protein coding gene will be correct in a future release.

Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
