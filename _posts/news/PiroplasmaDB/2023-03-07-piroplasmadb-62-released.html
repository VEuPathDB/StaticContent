New data in {{project}} 61

Genome sequence and annotation:

  • Babesia caballi USDA-D6B2 – Sequencing was performed using MinION. GCA_024862765.1.
  • Babesia ovis Selcuk – Sequencing was performed using MinION. GCA_023705535.1.
  • Theileria orientalis Fish Creek – Yam et al. 2022 and GCA_003072545.3.
  • Theileria orientalis Goon Nure – Yam et al. 2022 and GCA_003072535.3.

Genome Updates:

  • Babesia bovis T2Bo – The assembly was updated to the current INSDC version which was reassembled and reannotated. Ueti et al. 2021 . GCA_000165395.2.

Annotation Updates:

  • Cytauxzoon felis Winnie – Product descriptions for C. felis Winnie genes were transferred via orthology by VEuPathDB curators. all genes in C felis Winnie.
