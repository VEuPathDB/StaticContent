Forthcoming Reference Genome Transition and Request for Feedback

Recent updates to 4 mosquito and 1 tick species include mature assemblies that are much less fragmented or assembled to the chromosome level. Over the next 12 months VectorBase will transtion to these improved assemblies as reference for all functional data alignments in these species. During the first 6 months, functional data will be aligned to both old and new assemblies. During the second 6 months, functional data alignments to the old assembly will be removed but the old assembly will remain available for comparison. At the end of 12 months, the old assembly will also be removed. If you have any feedback, questions, need help, or are not able to find your genes of interest in the new genomes, please do not hesitate to contact us https://vectorbase.org/vectorbase/app/contact-us.

New data in {{project}} 62

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Dermacentor andersoni qqDerAnde1 – This isolate of Rocky Mountain wood tick was loaded from RefSeq which was annotated by the NCBI Eukaryotic Genome Annotation Pipeline. GCF_023375885.1.
  • Haemaphysalis longicornis HaeL-2018 – Tick isolate from Shandong (122.32°E, 36.89°N) and sequenced using Illumina, PacBio and HiC methods. Jia et al. 2020 and GCA_013339765.2.
  • Hyalomma asiaticum Hyas-2018 – Tick isolate from Guangxi (109.96°E, 22.41°N) and sequenced using Illumina, PacBio and HiC methods. Jia et al. 2020 and GCA_013339685.2.
  • Ixodes persulcatus Iper-2018 – Tick isolate from Heilongjiang (129.22°E, 44.96°N) and sequenced using Illumina, PacBio and HiC methods. Jia et al. 2020 and GCA_013358835.2.

RNA Sequence

Hixson et al. 2022

Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
