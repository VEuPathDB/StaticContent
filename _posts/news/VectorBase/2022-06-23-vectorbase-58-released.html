New features in {{project}} 58

'Send To' actions for the results of a gene search

  • Send to My Data Sets: Save your search result as an ID list in My Data Sets. Open the 'Send To' dropdown menu and choose 'My Data Sets' to create your private data set with the IDs from the search result. Your new data set record will appear on your My Data Sets page as a downloadable text file. You must be a registered {{project}} user and logged in to use this feature.

  • Send to VEuPathDB: Transfer a gene list from {{project}} to VEuPathDB and analyze in context with all VEuPathDB organisms. From the {{project}} gene result, open the 'Send To' dropdown menu and choose 'VEuPathDB.org Strategy'. The VEuPathDB My Strategies page will open in a new window displaying a one step strategy that returns the gene list just transferred from {{project}}. Once transferred, use the 'Add a step' tool to extend the strategy and take advantage of orthology across all VEuPathDB organsims.

Upload a gene list to My Data Sets

  • In preparation to provide gene set functionality such as gene set enrichment analysis we are introducing this new feature. The 'New upload' tab of My Data Sets allows for the upload of a gene list into {{project}} directly from a file or URL. Your new list will appear in the All tab and will remain available between sessions. If the results of your last experiment or the last article you read included a gene list, you can upload it here for further analysis.

Sequence Retrieval Tool improvements

  • The Sequence Retrieval Tool now remembers your parameter settings during your session. You can leave and return to the tool without having to set every parameter again.

New data in {{project}} 58

Genome updates

Anopheles stephensi UCISS2018
  • The nuclear genome was originally integrated in Release 53. The mitochondrial genome was added in Release 58.
  • Stable IDs were assigned to genes. New IDs, (e.g. ASTEI20_038720) begin with an organism abbreviation. Old IDs are mapped in VectorBase and listed in the Names, Previous Identifiers, and Aliases table. Searching for an old ID such as LOC118508044, will return the new ID ASTEI20_038720.

RNA Sequence

  • Gut transcriptomes of ISARL and GFP-injected ticks after engorgement on B. burgdorferi-infected or uninfected mice – Gut transcriptomes of ds I. scapularis adiponectin receptor-like (ISARL, ISCW018477) and ds Aequorea victoria green fluorescent protein (GFP -control)-injected ticks after 96 hr feeding/engorgement on Borrelia burgdorferi N40-infected or uninfected mice.Tang et al. 2021
  • Non-blood-feeding and post-blood-feeding female antennae transcriptomes – Non-blood-feeding (NBF) and post-blood-feeding (PBF) antennae (olfactory system) from female mosquitoes 3-5 days after eclosion were RNA-sequenced. Gu et al. 2022

Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
