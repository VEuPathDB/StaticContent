New data in {{project}} 59

Genome Updates

  • Culex quinquefasciatus JHB 2020 – now serves as the VectorBase reference for functional data originating from any strain of Culex quinquefasciatus.

Genome Sequence

  • Triatoma infestans FIOC_28 – the major insect vector of Chagas disease (American Trypanosomiasis). PRJNA589079

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Ixodes scapularis PalLabHiFi – the deer tick or black-legged tick sequenced with PacBio methods. PRJNA678334

RNA Sequence

  • Transcriptomic atlas of sugar- or blood-fed adult female body parts and gut regions – Aedes aegypti transcriptomic atlas of body parts (whole body, head, thorax, abdomen, ovary) and gut regions (gut, crop, proventriculus, anterior midgut, posterior midgut, hindgut, Malpighian tubules) in sugar-fed and blood-fed adult females. Hixson et al. 2022
  • Transcriptome of gut regions in sugar-fed adult females – Anopheles gambiae transcriptomic atlas of some gut regions in sugar-fed adult females (gut, proventriculus, anterior midgut, and posterior midgut. Hixson et al. 2022
  • Gut transcriptome of unfed ticks injected with recombinant mouse adiponectin – Transcriptional alterations in the unfed tick gut in the presence of mammalian adiponectin. Mouse recombinant adinopectin was injected.Tang et al. 2021
