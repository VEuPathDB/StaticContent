We are pleased to announce the update of four {{project}} genomes

The three genomes with structural annotations include new genes, modification of exisitng genes, or gene mergers and splits. The functional annotations include gene name/symbol and productdescription/putative function.
  • Anopheles coluzzii Ngousso AcolN1.1 ---> AcolN1.2
  • Anopheles quadriannulatus SANGWE AquaS1.12 ---> AquaS1.13
  • Glossina morsitans Yale GmorY1.11, no gene set change for this release, only functional annotations (structural annotations were added on the previous release for this genome, click here for details).
  • Rhodnius prolixus CDC RproC3.4 ---> RproC3.5

This is our second VEuPathDB community annotation integration, which was scheduled for Release 55 (November 2021). We incorporated ~nnn structural changes and over nnn thirds were attributed to Daniel Lawson. All the functional annotations are from Billiah Bwana (Kenya Agricultural and Livestock Research Organisation, KALRO), her huge contribution expanded the current and previous release.
