We are pleased to announce the update of four {{project}} genomes: three with structural annotations and one with fucntional annotations

  • Anopheles coluzzii Ngousso AcolN1.1 ---> AcolN1.2
  • Anopheles quadriannulatus SANGWE AquaS1.12 ---> AquaS1.13
  • Glossina morsitans Yale GmorY1.11, no gene set change for this release, only functional annotations (structural annoations were added on the previous release for this gneome, click here for details).
  • Rhodnius prolixus CDC RproC3.4 ---> RproC3.5

This is our second VEuPathDB community annotation integration, which was scheduled for Release 55 (November 2021). We incorporated ~nnn structural changes and over nnn thirds were attributed to Daniel Lawson. All the functional annotations are from Billiah Bwana (Kenya Agricultural and Livestock Research Organisation, KALRO), her huge contribution expanded the current and previous relase. See a breakdown of the changes and the data incorporated.
