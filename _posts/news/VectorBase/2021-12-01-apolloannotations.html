We are pleased to announce the update of four {{project}} genomes

The three genomes with structural annotations include new genes, modification of exisitng genes, and gene mergers and splits, meaning a change in the gene set nomenclature:
  • Anopheles coluzzii Ngousso AcolN1.1 ---> AcolN1.2
  • Anopheles quadriannulatus SANGWE AquaS1.12 ---> AquaS1.13
  • Rhodnius prolixus CDC RproC3.4 ---> RproC3.5

  • Glossina morsitans Yale GmorY1.11

This is our second VEuPathDB community annotation integration, which was scheduled for Release 55 (November 2021).
