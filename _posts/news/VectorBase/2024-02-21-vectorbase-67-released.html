  • Lutzomyia longipalpis M1 – A vector of Leishmania parasites that cause visceral leishmaniasis. A single male (M1) specimen of Lutzomyia longipalpis, derived from the Jacobina strain was sequenced using PacBio HiFi and Illumina HiC technologies. GCF_024334085.1;
