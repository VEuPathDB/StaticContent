New features in {{project}} 64

  • Download Data Files BETA – {{project}} has a new tool for downloading genome scale files such as genome.fasta or GFF files. Similar to other {{project}} tools, all data and files are available by default. To limit the result, choose a value (right) for one or more categories (left). From the filtered list, download a single file by clicking the file name, or multiple files using the check boxes and 'Dowload selected files' button. View Download Beta tutorial GIF.

Forthcoming Reference Genome Transition and Request for Feedback

Recent updates to 5 mosquito and 1 tick species include new assemblies that are much less fragmented, some organized to the chromosome level. As previously announced, VectorBase is on a 12-month transition period, to move these improved assemblies as reference for all functional data alignments in these species. In the first 6 months, functional data will be aligned to both old and new assemblies. During the second 6 months, functional data alignments to the old assembly will be removed but the old assembly will remain available for comparison. At the end of 12 months, the old assembly will also be removed. If you have any feedback or questions, need help, or are not able to find your genes of interest in the new genomes, please do not hesitate to contact us https://vectorbase.org/vectorbase/app/contact-us.

Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
