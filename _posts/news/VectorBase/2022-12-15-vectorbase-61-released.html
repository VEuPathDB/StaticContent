Forthcoming Reference Genome Transition and Request for Feedback

Recent updates to 4 mosquito and 1 tick species include mature assemblies that are much less fragmented or assembled to the chromosome level. Over the next 12 months VectorBase will transtion to these improved assemblies as reference for all functional data alignments in these species. During the first 6 months, functional data will be aligned to both old and new assemblies. During the second 6 months, functional data alignments to the old assembly will be removed but the old assembly will remain available for comparison. At the end of 12 months, the old assembly will also be removed. If you have any feedback, questions, need help, or are not able to find your genes of interest in the new genomes, please do not hesitate to contact us https://vectorbase.org/vectorbase/app/contact-us.
