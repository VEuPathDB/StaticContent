New features in {{project}} 62

  • Site search 'Reference Only' organism filter – Large results (> 50,000 hits) from the site search are common since it accesses all {{project}} data/page categories (genes, data types, data sets, organisms, etc.). The new 'Reference Only' toggle offers a one-click filter to reduce the result from all organisms to include only results from VEuPathDB reference orgnanisms.
  • Search details and data sets – Details for the search and the data sets accessed by the search are now available in tabbed pages from the top of the search pages. Previous versions of {{project}} presented this information at the bottom of the page where it was easily missed.
  • Scroll and zoom on gene page browser frames – The dynamic browser views on gene pages now provide active scroll and zoom functions with a reset button to return to the default view. Dynamic browser views are available for Synteny, Alignments, SNPs, Transcriptomics Coverage, Protein Features and Proteomics.

Forthcoming Reference Genome Transition and Request for Feedback

Recent updates to 4 mosquito and 1 tick species include new assemblies that are much less fragmented, some organized to the chromosome level. As previously announced, VectorBase is on a 12-month transition period, to move these improved assemblies as reference for all functional data alignments in these species. In the first 6 months, functional data will be aligned to both old and new assemblies. During the second 6 months, functional data alignments to the old assembly will be removed but the old assembly will remain available for comparison. At the end of 12 months, the old assembly will also be removed. If you have any feedback or questions, need help, or are not able to find your genes of interest in the new genomes, please do not hesitate to contact us https://vectorbase.org/vectorbase/app/contact-us.

New data in {{project}} 62

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Dermacentor andersoni qqDerAnde1 – This isolate of Rocky Mountain wood tick was loaded from RefSeq which was annotated by the NCBI Eukaryotic Genome Annotation Pipeline. GCF_023375885.1.
  • Haemaphysalis longicornis HaeL-2018 – Tick isolate from Shandong (122.32°E, 36.89°N) and sequenced using Illumina, PacBio and HiC methods. Jia et al. 2020 and GCA_013339765.2.
  • Hyalomma asiaticum Hyas-2018 – Tick isolate from Guangxi (109.96°E, 22.41°N) and sequenced using Illumina, PacBio and HiC methods. Jia et al. 2020 and GCA_013339685.2.
  • Ixodes persulcatus Iper-2018 – Tick isolate from Heilongjiang (129.22°E, 44.96°N) and sequenced using Illumina, PacBio and HiC methods. Jia et al. 2020 and GCA_013358835.2.

Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
