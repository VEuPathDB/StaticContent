New data in {{project}} 68

Transcriptomics

  • Cytoadherent and wild-type Plasmodium knowlesi (experiment 1) – RNA Seq transcriptome analysis of Plasmodium knowlesi lines with increased iRBC cytoadhesion activity including 4-, 8-, and 24-hr samples of Pan 0, 6, 8 and Pan 13 from Expt 2. Chuang et al. 2022.
  • Cytoadherent and wild-type Plasmodium knowlesi (experiment 2) – RNA Seq transcriptome analysis of Plasmodium knowlesi lines with increased iRBC cytoadhesion activity including 4-, 8-, and 24-hr samples of Pan 0 and Pan 13. Chuang et al. 2022.

Phenotypes

  • Plasmodium knowlesi essential genes via piggyBac screen – Plasmodium knowlesi essential genome determined by high density transposon insertion mutagenesis. need publication.
  • Plasmodium knowlesi piggyBAC Insertion Sites – JBrowse track showing coverage of sequence retrieved from quantitative insertion metagenesis sequencing (QiSeq). Peaks represent piggyBac insertion sites.need publication.
  • Teoretical TATA sites – JBrowse track showing every location of the sequence TATA in the Plasmodium knowlesi genome. need publication.

New features in {{project}} 68

  • The Sequence Retrieval Tool offers more input options such as uploading from a text file or URL. New options for configuring or choosing the type of sequence returned are also available.
  • The Product Descriptions table on gene pages is now called All Product Descriptions and contains product descriptions from all relevant sources. The most commonly used product description also appears at the top of the gene page.
  • The Text Search 'Fields' parameter values have changed. Choose 'Product descriptions (all)' to search the All Product Descriptions table on gene pages. Choose 'Product descriptions' to search only the commonly used product descriptions that appear at the top of gene pages.
  • The Organisms parameter in the Identify Genes based on SNP Characteristics search has a new look. Choose your organism of interest to load the Set of Samples parameter with that organism's WGS isolates.
  • Compressed files in the Download Data Files tool are now in .gz format.
