New data in {{project}} 57

Transcriptomics

  • High resolution intraerythrocytic time course transcriptome by RNA-Seq – Plasmodium falciparum 3D7 52 hour time course transcriptome (RNAseq) with samples taken at 2 hour intervals after parasites were double-synchronized with 5% sorbitol solution. Kucharski et al. 2020.
  • IDC in SR10KO and WT (2020) – Time-series RNA-seq analysis of 14 time-points from wild-type and sr10ko P. chabaudi parasites during the blood stage development. Subudhi et al. 2020.

New features in {{project}} 57

New 'Unannotated Intron Junctions' Search

  • The new Unannotated Intron Junctions search enables users to identify genes that contain, or are flanked by, unannotated high confidence intron junction-spanning reads from RNA-seq data. These genes may be incompletely or inaccurately annotated due to missing introns/exons and/or alternative splice variants. See our PDF Tutorial or our Video Tutorial for more information.
