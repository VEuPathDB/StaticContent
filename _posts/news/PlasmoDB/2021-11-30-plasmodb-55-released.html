New data in {{project}} 55

Genome Sequence

  • Plasmodium vivax PvSY56 – Draft genome of the chimpanzee Pan troglodytes ellioti infective P. vivax strain PvSY56 Loy et al. 2018.

Genome Sequence and Annotation

The following annotated genomes reported in Moser et al. 2020 appear for the first time in PlasmoDB. De novo genome assemblies from PacBio and Illumina generated sequence were annotated using Plasmodium falciparum 3D7 as a reference.
  • Plasmodium falciparum NF54 – isolated in 1979 from venous blood of a human in the Netherlands.
  • Plasmodium falciparum 7G8 2019 – isolated in 1980 from venous blood of a human in Brazil.
  • Plasmodium falciparum NF166 – isolated in 2010 from venous blood of a human in the Guinea.
  • Plasmodium falciparum NF135.C10 – isolated in 1993 from venous blood of a human in Cambodia.

Transcriptomics

  • Plasmodium falciparum
    • IDC in constant temperature and darkness (2020) – RNA seq analysis of intraerythrocytic development cycle in P. falciparum II3 cells. Duplicate samples taken at 2 hour intervals for 48 hours. Subudhi et al. 2020.
  • Plasmodium vinckei– The following data reported in Ramaprasad et al. 2021are aligned to Plasmodium vinckei CY, the PlasmoDB reference for P. vinckei strains. By aligning data for all strains against a single reference, all data are comparable.
    • Life cycle stage transcriptome for P. vinckei – RNA seq analysis of rings, trophozoites, gametocytes and mixed stages.
    • Life cycle stage transcriptome for P. vinckei petteri – RNA seq analysis of rings, trophozoites, gametocytes and mixed stages.
    • Mixed blood stage transcriptome for P. vinckei baforti EL – RNA seq analysis of mixed blood stages.
    • Mixed blood stage transcriptome for P. vinckei brucechwatti – RNA seq analysis of mixed blood stages.

Proteomics

  • Asexual stages ubiquitome – P. falciparum ubiquitome in four life stages: rings, schizonts, merozoites and trophozoites. Green et al. 2020.
    • Associated HostDB data: Human ubiquitome during infection with 4 stages of Plasmodium.
  • Oocyst sporozoite global proteome in P. falciparum – Proteomic analysis of Plasmodium falciparum oocyst sporozoites. These data first appeared in PlasmoDB 54 and have been updated with spectral counts in PlasmoDB 55. Lindner and Swearingen et al. 2020.
  • Oocyst sporozoite global proteome in P. yoelii – Proteomic analysis of Plasmodium yoelii oocyst sporozoites. These data first appeared in PlasmoDB 54 and have been updated with spectral counts in PlasmoDB 55. Lindner and Swearingen et al. 2020.
