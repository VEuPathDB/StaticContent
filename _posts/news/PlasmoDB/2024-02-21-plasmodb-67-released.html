VEuPathDB named as an Elixir Core Data Resource

Inducted in December 2023, VEuPathDB is honored to join the select group of Elixir Core Data Resources (CDR). With this distinction, VEuPathDB is identified as a resource of fundamental importance to the life science community and the preservation of biological data, dedicated to the principle of open access for publicly funded research. The CDR program recognizes resources that promote excellence and provide infrastructure vital for the advancement of the life sciences. Evaluation criteria include scientific focus, quality of science and community service, governance, impact, and ability to facilitate scientific research.

New data in {{project}} 67

Transcriptomics

The following RNA-Seq data sets are integrated and aligned to the PlasmoDB reference genome for Plasmodium yoelii – Plasmodium yoeliiyoelii 17X. For release 67, the data were also aligned to Plasmodium yoeliiyoelii 17XNL 2023.
  • Oocyst and salivary gland sporozoite transcriptomes in Plasmodium yoelii yoelii 17XNL – RNA sequence analysis of Plasmodium yoelii yoelii 17XNL salivary gland sporozoites and oocyst sporozoites. Data are aligned to Plasmodium yoelii yoelii 17XNL 2023. Lindner et al. 2019.
  • Salivary gland sporozoite transcriptomes: WT vs. Puf2-KO in Plasmodium yoelii yoelii 17XNL – Salivary gland sporozoite transcriptomes: WT vs. Puf2-KO mapped to Plasmodium yoelii yoelii 17XNL 2023. Lindner et al. 2013.

Proteomics

The following proteomics data sets are integrated and aligned to the PlasmoDB reference genome for Plasmodium yoelii – Plasmodium yoeliiyoelii 17X. For release 67, the data were also aligned to Plasmodium yoeliiyoelii 17XNL 2023.
  • Liver stage proteomes aligned to Plasmodium yoelii yoelii 17XNL 2023 – Proteomes of P yoeli 17NXL parasites isolated from infected mice at 40 and 50 hours post-infection. Tarun et al. 2008.
  • Oocyst sporozoite global proteome in P. yoelii yoelii 17XNL 2023 – Proteomic analysis of Plasmodium yoelii oocyst sporozoites. Lindner et al. 2019.
  • Salivary gland sporozoite total and surface proteome (17XNL2023) – P yoelii 17XNL sporozoite total and surface proteomes (2013) as well as PlasmoSEP surface proteomes (2016). Lindner et al. 2019.

New features in {{project}} 67

  • New options for downloading gene features are now available from any gene record page. Click the Download Gene link at the top of the record page to use the new BED and FASTA download options.
  • The {{project}} BETA version of long read RNA-Seq data support includes searches, Identify Genes based on Long Read Evidence and Identify Genomic Segments based on Long Read Evidence, and JBrowse tracks of read coverage for each sample in a data set.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
