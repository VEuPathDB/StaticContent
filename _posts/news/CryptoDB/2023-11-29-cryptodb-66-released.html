New data in {{project}} 66

Single-cell RNA Sequence

  • Cryptospordium parvum IowaII asexual stages cell atlas – Single-cell RNA sequencings of 2,989 Cryptosporidium parvum cells from culture reveals the temporal gene expression program of the asexual cycle. Provided pre-publication from the Striepen Lab
  • Cryptospordium parvum IowaII full cell atlas – Single-cell RNA sequencing of 9,098 Cryptosporidium parvum cells from culture and infected mice reveals the gene expression program across the entire parasite life cycle. Provided pre-publication from the Striepen Lab

Annotation Updates

  • Porospora gigantea A – Pfam domain names were substituted for product descriptions for genes with assigned Pfam domains and uninformative product descriptions such as 'unspecified product'. 2711 genes were given new product descriptions.
  • Porospora gigantea B – Pfam domain names were substituted for product descriptions for genes with assigned Pfam domains and uninformative product descriptions such as 'unspecified product'. 2765 genes were given new product descriptions.

New features in {{project}} 66

'On-Demand Training' provides embedded videos for special topic webinars. All webinar recordings are also available from the VEuPathDB YouTube Channel.
  • OrthoMCL DB offers a new preconfigured download table called Phyletic Distribution Clade Abundance that offers the OMCL group distribution of sequences in Alveolata, Archaea, Amoeba, Bacteria, Fungi, Euglenozoa, Metazoa, Viridiplantae, and other Eukaryotes. OMCL Download Tutorial
