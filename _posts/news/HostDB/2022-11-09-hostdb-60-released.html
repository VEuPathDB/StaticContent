New data in {{project}} 60

RNA Sequence

  • Babesia infection in dog – Canine tanscriptome during a controlled infection with Babesia rossi at low or high innoculum. RNA seq was performed on whole blood drawn at 0, 1, 3, 4, 6, and 8 days post-infection. Smith et al. 2021.
  • Chicken macrophage-like cells (HD11) infected in vitro with Eimeria tenella – Transcriptome of macrophage-like cell line HD11 cultures infected in vitro with purified E. tenella sporozoites. Control cultures as well as cultures harvested as 2, 4, 12, 24, 48 and 72 hours post-infection. Sandholt et al. 2021. The Eimeria tenella portion of this data is available in ToxoDB: Eimeria tenella transcriptome during infection in chicken macrophage-like cells.
  • Mouse transcriptome during Eimeria falciformis infection – Dual RNA sequencing of mice infected with Eimeria falciformis. RNA isolated from ceca from 3 to 4 sacrificed mice per time-point was sequenced and reads were aligned to Mus musculus C57BL6J. Ehret et al. 2017. The Eimeria falciformis portion of this data is available in ToxoDB: Eimeria transcriptome during infection in mice (Dual RNA-Seq)
  • IEC18 cells infected with Toxoplasma gondii – Rat intestinal epithelial cells (IEC18) infected with Toxoplasma gondii tachyzoites and sporozoites for 8hrs. Guiton et al. 2017. The Toxoplasma gondii portion of this data is available in ToxoDB: Toxoplasma gondii during infection in IEC18 cells

New features in {{project}} 60

{{project}} site search accesses Apollo data

  • Gene annotation updates entered in {{project}} Apollo are now indexed and available to the site search. Genes with Apollo annotations that contain your search term will be available in site search results under >Genes >Filter gene fields.

Gene page navigation improvements

  • The RNA-Seq Transcription Summary graph now links directly to the Transcript Expression table. Click a data set title in the RNA-Seq Transcription Summary graph to navigate directly to the details for the same data set in the Transcript Expression table.
  • The gene page content navigation tool now contains links to every table or attribute on the gene page. Open each numbered data section to reveal links to all data within the section. Or use the 'expand all' link at the top of the content tool to view all links.

AlphaFold data available

  • Protein structure predictions from AlphaFold are now available in {{project}}. The AlphaFold database (Version 3) was downloaded and structures were associated to {{project}} genes via the UniProt ID. Jumper et al. 2021 and Varadi et al. 2022
  • The AlphaFold search returns all genes with AlphaFold predictions in {{project}}.
  • Gene pages include a table of AlphaFold data and the AlphaFold 3D viewer for dynamic visualization of the predicted structure.
