  • Transcriptomics of human airway epithelial cells upon exposure to Aspergillus fumigatus – Aspergillus fumigatus (strains Af293 or CEA10) were exposed to human cells for 6 or 16 hours.RNA-seq analysis was performed on both the host and fungal samples. Watkins et al. 2018.

New features in {{project}} 66

  • The {{project}} webinars page has a new look. 'Upcoming' webinars announce the next few webinars. 'Releases' contains the recordings for our previous release webinars. 'On-Demand Training' provides embedded videos for special topic webinars. All webinar recordings are also available from the VEuPathDB YouTube Channel.
  • OrthoMCL DB offers a new preconfigured download table called Phyletic Distribution Clade Abundance that offers the OMCL group distribution of sequences in Alveolata, Archaea, Amoeba, Bacteria, Fungi, Euglenozoa, Metazoa, Viridiplantae, and other Eukaryotes. OMCL Download Tutorial


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
