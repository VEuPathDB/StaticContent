New data in {{project}} 65

Transcriptomics

Infection of human blood
  • Candida auris infection of human whole blood – Homo sapiens transcriptome from an ex vivo whole blood infection model during infection with Candida auris. Allert et al. 2022

Infection of human cell culture
  • Transcriptome dynamics in human cell culture during Candida albicans infection – Characterization of the host cell transcriptome during in vitro Candida albicans infection of human endothelial or oral epithelial cells. Liu et al. 2015.

Infections of mouse BMDMs
  • In vitro infections of mouse BMDMs with Rhizopus delemar – Mouse bone-marrow derived macrophages (BMDMs) were infected the pathogenic mold, Rhizopus delemar, for 1, 4 or 18 hours and then subjected to RNA-seq analysis to determine the transcriptional response of both the fungus and the host to infection. Andrianaki et al. 2018.
