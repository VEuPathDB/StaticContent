VEuPathDB named as an Elixir Core Data Resource

Inducted in December 2023, VEuPathDB is honored to join the select group of Elixir Core Data Resources (CDR). With this distinction, VEuPathDB is identified as a resource of fundamental importance to the life science community and the preservation of biological data, dedicated to the principle of open access for publicly funded research. The CDR program recognizes resources that promote excellence and provide infrastructure vital for the advancement of the life sciences. Evaluation criteria include scientific focus, quality of science and community service, governance, impact, and ability to facilitate scientific research.

Genome Sequence and Annotation

    • Transcriptomics

    Homo sapiens
    • Transcriptomic response of human alveolar epithelial cells exposed to Scedosporium aurantiacum conidia – Trascriptomics response of A549 cells to a virulent S. aurantiacum strain WM 06.482 in vitro. Kaur et al. 2019.
    • Enteroid-derived IEC infected with Giardia – Human portion of dual RNA Seq data during early stage infection (1 and 3 hr) of enteroid-derived IEC infected with Giardia intestinalis trophozoites. Grüttner et al. 2023. This data has associated data sets in GiardiaDB – Giardia transcriptome during infection in enteroid-derived IEC.
    Mus musculus
    • Characterization of transcriptome dynamics in response to contact with host cells – Murine transcriptomes in two different murine models of candidiasis (hematogenously disseminated candidiasis(HDC) and oropharyngeal candidiasis (OPC)). Liu et al. 2015.

    Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
