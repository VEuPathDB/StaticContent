New data in {{project}} 53

Transcriptomics

  • Mouse transcriptome during CY infection time course – Four days after initiating Plasmodium vinckei CY infections in CBA mice, blood was collected via tail snip at 6, 12, 18 and 24 hours. Samples were analyzed by Illumina mRNA sequencing from total RNA Ramaprasad et al. 2019.
    • Associated PlasmoDB data: Intraerythrocytic development cycle for P. vinckei CY.
  • In vivo mouse transcriptome during Plasmodium chaubadi infection – Mouse RNA-Seq transcriptome during infection with Plasmodium chabaudi WT or SR10 knockout. Samples collected by micro-sampling and terminal artery bleed. Ramaprasad et al. 2019.
    • Associated PlasmoDB data: In vivo transcriptome in mouse

New features in {{project}} 53

  • Genome data set records ({{project}} genome data sets) are updated to increase clarity and reduce redundancy of information on the page. For example, a new table "Explore other Categories of Data Associated with this Genome" contains links to all functional data sets for the organism. The new "Data Context" section provides counts for genes, assembled sequences and transcripts. Functional data set records have received similar updates.
  • The site search, available in the header, is updated to find versions of an ID (e.g. ID.1, ID.2). Specifically, searching for the ID will retrieve ID, ID.1 and ID.2, etc, while searching for ID.1 will retrieve only ID.1
  • The community annotation software, Apollo, was updated to version 2.6.5 (About Apollo).
  • The BLAT sequence search tool is integrated into Apollo. Use the 'Search' tab from the annotator panel to find your sequence of interest in our Apollo-available genomes. (ApolloBLAT.png)


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary.
