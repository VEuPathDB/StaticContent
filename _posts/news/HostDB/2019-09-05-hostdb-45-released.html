New data in HostDB 45

Transcriptomics

  • Transcriptional analysis of sorted subpopulations of mouse macrophages infected with C. albicans — C57BL6J macrophages were infected with the CAI4 strain of Candida albicans. Muñoz et al. 2019.

New features in HostDB 45

  • All EuPathDB sites now offer the JBrowse Genome Browser. GBrowse will be phased out in coming releases. Please Contact Us with comments and suggestions for the genome browser.
  • The new RNASeq Transcript Summary tool on gene pages helps reveal experiments in which the gene is highly regulated. The tool displays the gene's FPKM values for all experiments in HostDB and offers features such as log/linear scaling, zoom and pan to explore the gene's expression in each experiment.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
