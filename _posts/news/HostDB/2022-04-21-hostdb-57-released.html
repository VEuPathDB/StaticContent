New data in {{project}} 57

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Canis lupus familiaris isolate SID07034 – Long-read based reference genome assembly of a male Labrador retriever dog. Integrated from Ensembl Rapid Release.
  • Gallus gallus isolate bGalGal1 – Long-read based chicken reference genome assembly of a broiler mother and white leghorn layer father. Integrated from NCBI-Ref Seq.
  • Rattus norvegicus BN/NHsdMcwi – Long-read based reference genome assembly for the Norway rat. Integrated from Ensembl Rapid Release.

RNA Sequence

  • Chicken caecal tissue during Eimeria tenella infection – Transcriptome of chicken caecal tissues collected during infection in vivo with purified E. tenella sporozoites. RNA-Seq was performed on samples collected from uninfected controls as well as cultures 1, 2, 3, 4, and 10 days post-infection Sandholt et al. 2021. The pathogen-aligned data analysis of this dual RNA seq dataset appears in ToxoDB: Transcriptome of E. tenella from infected chicken caecal tissues.
  • Chicken macrophage-like cells (HD11) infected in vitro with Eimeria tenella – Transcriptome of macrophage-like cell line HD11 cultures infected in vitro with purified E. tenella sporozoites. Control cultures as well as cultures harvested as 2, 4, 12, 24, 48 and 72 hours post-infection Sandholt et al. 2021. The pathogen-aligned data analysis of this dual RNA seq dataset appears in ToxoDB: Eimeria tenella transcriptome during infection in chicken macrophage-like cells.
  • IFN-gamma independent host response to intestinal C. parvum infection – RNA Seq transcriptome of the ileum and cecum of mice infected with Cryptosporidium parvum during treatment with STAg. Infection and treatment controls included. Gallego-Lopez et al. 2022. The pathogen-aligned data analysis of this dual RNA seq dataset appears in CryptoDB: Infected ileum and cecum of IFN-gamma negative, STAg treated mice.
  • Rat brain primary cells transcriptome during infection with T. gondii – Rat primary brain cell response to T. gondii infection during parasite spontaneous differentiation - triplicate RNA samples of infected primary CNS cell culture at 1d, 2d, 4d, 7d, and 14d post-infection, as well as uninfected controls. Mouveaux et al. 2021. The pathogen-aligned data analysis of this dual RNA seq dataset appears in ToxoDB: Infected ileum and cecum of IFN-gamma negative, STAg treated mice.
