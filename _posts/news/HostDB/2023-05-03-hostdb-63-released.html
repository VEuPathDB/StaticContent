New data in {{project}} 63

RNA Sequence

Candida albicans infection in mouse
  • RNA-seq analysis of an in vivo murine model of vulvovaginal candidiasis – Murine vaginas were infected with Candida albicans and harvested for RNA-seq analysis 3 days post-infection. Bruno et al. 2015
Rhizopus delemar indection in mouse
  • Mouse transcriptional response during early stages of R. delemar infection (pulmonary mucormycosis) – Transcriptional response of mouse lungs to infection with Rhizopus delemar. Watkins et al. 2018

New features in {{project}} 63

  • Download Data Files BETA – {{project}} has a new tool for downloading genome scale files such as genome.fasta or GFF files.
