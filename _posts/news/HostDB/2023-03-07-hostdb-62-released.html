New data in {{project}} 61

RNA Sequence

Candida species infections in mouse
  • Macrophage responses to Candida infection – Transcriptional responses during macrophage phagocytosis of Candida albicans SC5314, Candida dubliniensis CD36, Candida parapsilosis CDC317, Candida tropicalis MYA-3404, Clavispora lusitaniae ATCC 6260, and Lodderomyces elongisporus ATCC 42720. Pountain et al. 2021. This HostDB data set has associated data sets.

Giardia Assemblage A co-infections with human
  • Human IEC transcriptome during coincubation with Giardia cysts – RNA-seq data from human IEC (Caco-2 cells) during a coincubation with Giardia cysts. Mixed parasite and host samples collected at 1.5, 3 and 4.5 hours and host control cells were also assayed. Rojas et al. 2022. This HostDB data has associated data sets.
  • Human transcriptome during infection with encysting Giardia – Transcriptome of human Caco-2 cells infected with Giardia intestinalis WB trohozoites 7h into encystation. Rojas et al. 2022. This HostDB data has associated data sets.
  • Human IEC cell transcriptome during Giardia infection – Host gene expression changes during Giardia intenstinalis infection of human intestinal epithelial cells (differentiated Caco-2 cells). Three biological replicates of control and 1.5hr, 3hr, and 4.5hr time points measured were analyzed by RNA sequencing. Rojas et al. 2022. This HostDB data has associated data sets.
