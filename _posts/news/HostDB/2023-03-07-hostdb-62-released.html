New data in {{project}} 62

RNA Sequence

Candida albicans
  • Mouse transcriptomes during oropharyngeal candidiasis infection – The experimental model of oropharyngeal candidiasis (OPC) in mice is used to understand the interaction of the fungal pathogen C. albicans with the host at mucosal surfaces. Mouse was infected with Candida albicans isolates SC5314 and 101 and gene expression was studied using RNA sequencing and analysis Kirchner et al. 2019. This HostDB data set has associated data sets.
