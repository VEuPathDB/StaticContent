New data in {{project}} 58

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Cavia porcellus 2N – Annotated genome of the guinea-pig. PRJNA12583.

RNA Sequence

  • mRNA-based anti-tick (I. scapularis) vaccine – Guinea pigs were inmmunized with 19ISP, an mRNA vaccine encoding 19 I. scapularis salivary proteins. Differential host transcriptional response was measured in blood 2 weeks after the last vaccination booster. Sajid et al. 2021.
  • T gondii infected human host cells vs control – Transcriptome of Toxoplasma gondii RH infected and control human foreskin fibroblasts determined by RNA Seq. PRJNA316438.
  • Transcriptional profile of bone marrow derived dendritic cells in response to infection by Paracoccidioides – Examination and comparison of the transcriptional profile of bone marrow derived dendritic cells (BMDCs) in response to infection by the fungus Paracoccidioides brasiliensis in resistant/susceptible mice. de-Souza-Silva et al. 2020.

ChIP Sequence

  • Cis-regulatory landscape of T gondii infected human host cells – Open chromatin cis-regulatory elements present in uninfected and T. gondii (Type I-RH) infected human foreskin fibroblasts. Determined by ATAC seq. PRJNA316437 and Wijetunga et al. 2017.
