New data in {{project}} 58

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Canis lupus familiaris isolate SID07034 – Long-read based reference genome assembly of a male Labrador retriever dog. Integrated from Ensembl Rapid Release.
  • Gallus gallus isolate bGalGal1 – Long-read based chicken reference genome assembly of a broiler mother and white leghorn layer father. Integrated from NCBI-Ref Seq.
  • Rattus norvegicus BN/NHsdMcwi – Long-read based reference genome assembly for the Norway rat. Integrated from Ensembl Rapid Release.

RNA Sequence

  • Chicken caecal tissue during Eimeria tenella infection – Transcriptome of chicken caecal tissues collected during infection in vivo with purified E. tenella sporozoites. RNA-Seq was performed on samples collected from uninfected controls as well as cultures 1, 2, 3, 4, and 10 days post-infection Sandholt et al. 2021. The pathogen-aligned data analysis of this dual RNA seq dataset appears in ToxoDB: Transcriptome of E. tenella from infected chicken caecal tissues.
  • Transcriptional profile of bone marrow derived dendritic cells in response to infection by Paracoccidioides – Examination and comparison of the transcriptional profile of bone marrow derived dendritic cells (BMDCs) in response to infection by the fungus Paracoccidioides brasiliensis in resistant/susceptible mice. de-Souza-Silva et al. 2020.
