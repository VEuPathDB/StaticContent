--- layout: post title: OrthoMCL 6.12 released category: [OrthoMCL,VEuPathDB] --- OrthoMCL Version 6.12 is released. In this release, 24 Peripheral organisms were added. Thus, OrthoMCL now predicts orthology for a total of 745 organisms (595 Peripheral and 150 Core organisms). Proteins from the 12 new organisms were mapped into Core ortholog groups. Then, a new set of Residual ortholog groups (e.g. OG6r12_100018) were formed from the collection of all unmapped Peripheral proteins. See the About page for the methods.
