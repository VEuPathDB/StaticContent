--- layout: post title: OrthoMCL 6.10 released category: [OrthoMCL,VEuPathDB] --- OrthoMCL Version 6.10 is released. In this release, 18 Peripheral organisms were added. Thus, OrthoMCL now predicts orthology for a total of 695 organisms (545 Peripheral and 150 Core organisms). Proteins from the 22 new organisms were mapped into Core ortholog groups. Then, a new set of Residual ortholog groups (e.g. OG6r9_100018) were formed from the collection of all unmapped Peripheral proteins. See the About page for the methods.
