--- layout: post title: OrthoMCL 6.21 released category: [OrthoMCL,VEuPathDB] ---

OrthoMCL Version 6.21 is released. In this release, 16 Peripheral organisms were added. Thus, OrthoMCL now predicts orthology for a total of 857 organisms (707 Peripheral and 150 Core organisms). Proteins from the 16 new organisms were mapped into Core ortholog groups. Then, a new set of Residual ortholog groups were formed from the collection of all unmapped Peripheral proteins. See the About page for the methods.

For OrthoMCL build 6.21, the Galaxy workflow that maps user proteomes to OrthoMCL groups has been updated, replacing NCBI BLASTP with Diamond blastp. Diamond has approximately 99.8% of the sensitivity of Blast, but runs 1000-fold faster. Jobs that took 4-12 hours with BLAST, can now run in 2-10 minutes. The output from the new workflow is identical in format to the BLAST based workflow. The original BLAST workflow is still available.
