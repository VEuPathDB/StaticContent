--- layout: post title: OrthoMCL 6.6 released category: [OrthoMCL,VEuPathDB] --- OrthoMCL Version 6.6 is released. In this release, 19 Peripheral species were added. Thus, OrthoMCL now predicts orthology for a total of 650 organisms (500 Peripheral and 150 Core organisms). Proteins from the 19 new species were mapped into Core ortholog groups. Then, a new set of Residual ortholog groups (e.g. OG6r6_103449) were formed from the collection of all unmapped Peripheral proteins. See the About page for the methods.
