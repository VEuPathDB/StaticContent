--- layout: post title: OrthoMCL 6.20 released category: [OrthoMCL,VEuPathDB] ---

OrthoMCL Version 6.20 is released. In this release, 16 Peripheral organisms were added. Thus, OrthoMCL now predicts orthology for a total of 857 organisms (707 Peripheral and 150 Core organisms). Proteins from the 16 new organisms were mapped into Core ortholog groups. Then, a new set of Residual ortholog groups were formed from the collection of all unmapped Peripheral proteins. See the About page for the methods.

The BLAST database was updated on DATE to remove obsolete proteins and to add back ## proteomes of VEuPathDB organisms that have been inadvertantly missing since July 2023. Repeating BLAST searches run between July 2023 will provide the most accurate results.
