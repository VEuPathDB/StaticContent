--- layout: post title: OrthoMCL 6.7 released category: [OrthoMCL,VEuPathDB] --- OrthoMCL Version 6.7 is released. In this release, 5 Peripheral species were added. Thus, OrthoMCL now predicts orthology for a total of 655 organisms (505 Peripheral and 150 Core organisms). Proteins from the 5 new species were mapped into Core ortholog groups. Then, a new set of Residual ortholog groups (e.g. OG6r6_103449) were formed from the collection of all unmapped Peripheral proteins. See the About page for the methods.
