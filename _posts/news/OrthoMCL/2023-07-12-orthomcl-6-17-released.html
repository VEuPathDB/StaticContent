--- layout: post title: OrthoMCL 6.17 released category: [OrthoMCL,VEuPathDB] --- OrthoMCL Version 6.17 is released. In this release, 15 Peripheral organisms were added. Thus, OrthoMCL now predicts orthology for a total of 826 organisms (676 Peripheral and 150 Core organisms). Proteins from the 15 new organisms were mapped into Core ortholog groups. Then, a new set of Residual ortholog groups (e.g. OG6r17_129716) were formed from the collection of all unmapped Peripheral proteins. See the About page for the methods.
