--- layout: post title: OrthoMCL 6.19 released category: [OrthoMCL,VEuPathDB] --- OrthoMCL Version 6.19 is released. In this release, 9 Peripheral organisms were added. Thus, OrthoMCL now predicts orthology for a total of 841 organisms (691 Peripheral and 150 Core organisms). Proteins from the 9 new organisms were mapped into Core ortholog groups. Then, a new set of Residual ortholog groups were formed from the collection of all unmapped Peripheral proteins. See the About page for the methods.
