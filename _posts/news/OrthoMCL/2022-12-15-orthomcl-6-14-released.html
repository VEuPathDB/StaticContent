--- layout: post title: OrthoMCL 6.14 released category: [OrthoMCL,VEuPathDB] --- OrthoMCL Version 6.14 is released. In this release, 19 Peripheral organisms were added. Thus, OrthoMCL now predicts orthology for a total of 789 organisms (639 Peripheral and 150 Core organisms). Proteins from the 19 new organisms were mapped into Core ortholog groups. Then, a new set of Residual ortholog groups (e.g. OG6r14_100006) were formed from the collection of all unmapped Peripheral proteins. See the About page for the methods.
