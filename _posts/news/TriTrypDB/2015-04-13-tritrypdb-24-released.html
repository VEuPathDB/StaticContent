We are pleased to announce the release of TriTrypDB 24
All EuPathDB release numbers have been synchronized to help us more efficiently manage our expanding number of databases.  As of this release all EuPathDB resource release numbers have been set to release 24.  Subsequent version numbers will increase by one with each release.  For information about individual dataset version numbers see the Data Sets page.

       New data in this release:

The data set title below links to a full description and list of associated searches and genome browser tracks.

 

Genome:

Leishmania donovani BHU 1220 - first release in TriTrypDB (Neeloo Singh unpublished; Genbank version Nov 6, 2013)

 

Newly Annotated Genomes:

Genomes from the Trypanosomatid sequencing white paper project have been annotated in collaboration with The Sanger Institute's Parasite Genomics Group.  In this release the following newly annotated genomes are available:

Leishmania aethiopica L147

Leishmania arabica strain LEM1108

Leishmania braziliensis MHOM/BR/75/M2903

Leishmania enriettii strain LEM3045

Leishmania gerbilli strain LEM45

Leishmania panamensis MHOM/COL/81/L13

Leishmania turanica strain LEM423

Leishmania sp. MAR LEM2494

Leishmania tropica L590


Updated Functional Annotation:

Trypanosoma congolense IL3000 - updated: 7297 genes with updated products (GeneDB version Feb 19, 2015).


Crithidia fasciculata Cf-C1 - updated: 1887 genes with updated products (unassembled contigs; Parasite Genomics Group, Sanger Institute)


Proteomics:

Promastigote and amastigote stage proteomes (MHOM/IN/80/Dd8)   - L. donovani MHOM/IN/80/Dd8 promasatigote and amastigote stage proteomes determined by searching and mapping the L. donovani MHOM/IN/80/Dd8 mass-spec peptides against the non-redundant protein database of L. donovani BPK282A1. Nirujogi et al.

 

Promastigote Proteome (MHOM/IL/67/JERICHO II (ATCC 50122))  - L. major ATCC 50122 promastigote proteome determined by searching and mapping against Leishmania major strain Friedlin.  Pawar et al.

 

RNA Sequencing:

Procyclic and bloodstream form mRNA transcriptome and ribosome profiling - Ribosome profile and mRNA transcriptome of the T. brucei Lister 427 bloodstream and procyclic forms mapped to T. brucei TREU 927.  Vasquez et al.

 

Bloodstream and Procyclic for spliced leader transcriptomes (927, 427)(2014) - Spliced leader sites determined by RNA sequencing of SL sequence-primed cDNA libraries from in vivo T. brucei 927 procyclic and slender forms as well as a cultured T. brucei 427 bloodstream form.  Jensen et al.

 

Whole Genome Resequencing:

Copy number variations in resequenced strains of Leishmania braziliensis, Leishmania donovani, Leishmania infantum, Leishmania major, Leishmania Mexicana - Coverage from short-read DNA-seq data was used to infer ploidy and gene copy numbers in resequenced strains.  Rogers et al.
