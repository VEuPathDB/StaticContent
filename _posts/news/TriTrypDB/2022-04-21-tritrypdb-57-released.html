Updated Functional Annotation

Functional annotations in TriTrypDB 57 have been updated for the Trypanosoma brucei TREU927 genome.

Summary of annotation updates which include products, GO terms, gene names, publications and notes from curators. These functional annotations are current with GeneDB March 2022 GFF version. Functional annotation suggestions made by our users via the VEuPathDB Apollo instance are reviewed by our curators and are included in the official annotation when appropriate.
