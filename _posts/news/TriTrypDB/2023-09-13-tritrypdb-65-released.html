New data in {{project}} 65

Localization by LOPIT Mass Spec

  • Spatial proteomics of Trypanosoma brucei bloodstream and procyclic forms. – T. brucei BSF and PCF were analyzed by hyperLopit spatial proteomics to provide evidence of protein expression and assignment of subcellular location of thousands of proteins per cell-type. Moloney et al. 2023
  • Spatial proteomics of Trypanosoma congolense bloodstream and procyclic forms. – T. congolense BSF and PCF were analyzed by hyperLopit spatial proteomics to provide evidence of protein expression and assignment of subcellular location of thousands of proteins per cell-type. Moloney et al. 2023

Functional annotations in TriTrypDB 65 have been updated for the T. brucei 927. Summary of annotation updates which include products, GO terms, gene names, synonyms and PMID.

New features in {{project}} 65

  • The {{project}} webinars page has a new look. 'Upcoming' webinars announce the next few webinars. 'Releases' contains the recordings for our previous release webinars. 'On-Demand Training' provides embedded videos for special topic webinars. All webinar recordings are also available from the VEuPathDB YouTube Channel.
  • OrthoMCL DB offers a new preconfigured download table called Phyletic Distribution Clade Abundance that offers the OMCL group distribution of sequences in Alveolata, Archaea, Amoeba, Bacteria, Fungi, Euglenozoa, Metazoa, Viridiplantae, and other Eukaryotes. OMCL Download Tutorial
