New data in {{project}} 66

Transcriptomics

  • Trypanosoma cruzi life stages transcriptomes aligned to T. cruzi CL Brener Esmeraldo-like – RNA-seq of different stages of the parasite life cycle - epimastigotes, intracellular amastigotes and cell-derived trypomastigotes. Díaz-Viraqué et al. 2023
  • Trypanosoma cruzi life stages transcriptomes aligned to T. cruzi Dm28c 2018 – RNA-seq of different stages of the parasite life cycle - epimastigotes, intracellular amastigotes and cell-derived trypomastigotes. RNA-seq reads are aligned to the T. cruzi Dm28c 2018 genome. Díaz-Viraqué et al. 2023

ChIP-Seq

  • Distribution of 5mC and 6mA in epimastigotes and trypomastigotes – Genomic distribution of 5mC and 6mA in epimastigotes and cell-derived trypomastigotes. Díaz-Viraqué et al. 2023

JBrowse Tracks

  • Core and disruptive genome compartments in T cruzi Dm28c – Díaz-Viraqué et al. 2023
  • Core and disruptive genome compartments in Trypanosoma cruzi Brazil A4 – Chromosomal positions of core and disruptive genome compartments in T cruzi Brazil A4. Díaz-Viraqué et al. 2023

New features in {{project}} 66

  • New options for downloading gene features are now availalbe from any gene record page. Click the Download Gene link at the top of the record page to use the new BED and FASTA download options.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data September slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
