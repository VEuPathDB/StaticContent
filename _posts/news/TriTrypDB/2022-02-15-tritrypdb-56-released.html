New data in {{project}} 56

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Trypanosoma brucei EATRO1125 – an isolate that is used increasingly often because it is pleomorphic in mammals and fly transmissible, two characteristics that have been lost by the most commonly used laboratory stocks. Naguleswaran et al. 2021

RNA Sequence

  • Effect of heat shock on promastigote transcriptome – Transcriptome of Leishmania major Friedlin during mild heat shock, 26℃ vs 37℃. Rastrojo et al. 2019
  • Life Stages Transcriptomes (AMA, META, PRO) – Transcriptome profiling of L. braziliensis throughout its life cycle was performed using RNA-seq technology. The total RNA of three biological replicates from the three main life stages of L. braziliensis was sequenced, resulting in nine cDNA libraries. Ruy et al. 2019
  • Promastigote transcriptome for E monterogeii ATCC30507 – RNA Seq transcriptome of promastigote stage from Endotrypanum monterogeii strain ATCC30507 Albanaz et al. 2021

New features in {{project}} 56

  • My Organism Preferences is a new option for filtering menus to a subset of organisms in {{project}}. Use the My Organism Preferences page to choose the organisms you are interested in. {{project}} will function as if the database contained only your chosen organisms. The feature is easily disabled/enabled at any time from a button located under the header on every page. More Details
  • The Reference sequence track in JBrowse has a new color scheme. View a tutorial GIF.
    • Purines appear in shades of blue while pyrimidines appear in shades of yellow.
    • Stop codons are red while methionines are green.
    • The six frame translations appear in gray.
    • Nucleotides and amino acids are distinguished by color at low zoom. Under high zoom they are also labeled with single letter codes.
  • Galaxy Version updated on 8 Oct, 2021: This updated version of the Galaxy software improves job and workflow performance. In addition to the previous set of tools and workflows, we now offer additional tools, such as those for scRNA-seq (Scanpy, Seurat), species ID (CryptoGenotyper), table manipulation (Datamash), and proteomics (MSstats, Search GUI, Peptide Shaker, MaxQuant). Certain tools have been upgraded and now function without errors: bamCoverage, bgzip, tabix and Upload File.
  • Enhanced searching on gene record pages – The content navigation tool now accesses detailed descriptions of {{project}} gene page content, making it easier to find the content that you need.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary.
