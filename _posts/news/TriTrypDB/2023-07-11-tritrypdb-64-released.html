New data in {{project}} 64

Genome Sequence

  • Blastocrithidia sp. p57 – Illumina and PacBio sequence assembly. Kachale et al. 2023 – GCA_028554745.1.
  • Trypanosoma congolense Tc1/148 – Silva Pereira et al. 2018 – GCA_002287245.1.

TrypTag data updated

  • Cellular localization of tagged T. brucei proteins in procyclic forms – The data were updated with mitochondrial proteome GO terms. The data are reflected in the 'Protein targeting and localization' and 'GO Terms' sections of the record pages of updated genes.

New features in {{project}} 64

  • Download Data Files BETA – {{project}} has a new tool for downloading genome scale files such as genome.fasta or GFF files. Similar to other {{project}} tools, all data and files are available by default. To limit the result, choose a value (right) for one or more categories (left). From the filtered list, download a single file by clicking the file name, or multiple files using the check boxes and 'Dowload selected files' button. View Download Beta tutorial GIF.
