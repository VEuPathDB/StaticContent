New data in {{project}} 59

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Leishmania major Friedlin 2021 – The L. major Friedlin genome was re-sequenced using the Pacific Biosciences (PacBio) technology, which provides long reads able to span long repeats, and the Illumina technology to generate paired-end short-reads useful to join fragmented chromosomes, extend chromosomes ends and correct homopolymer indel errors. Camacho et al. 2021
  • Trypanosoma equiperdum OVI – the causative agent of dourine, a sexually-transmitted infection of horses. Hébert et al. 2017

ChIP Sequence

  • ChIP-seq of Leishmania mexicana BDF5 – Bomodomain binding factor sites in Leishmania mexicana. Jones et al. 2022.

New features in {{project}} 59

{{project}} site search accesses Apollo data

  • Gene annotation updates entered in {{project}} Apollo are now indexed and available to the site search. Genes with Apollo annotations that contain your search term will be available in site search results under >Genes >Filter gene fields.

Single cell RNA-seq Βeta version

  • Single cell RNA-seq data is now represented in {{project}} via UMAP in cellxgene .
  • A new search that returns genes represented in scRNA-seq data sets is categorized under Search For, Genes, Transcriptomics, Single Cell RNA-Seq Evidence. Use this search to retrieve a list of all genes with expression in a particular scRNA-seq data set, as well as a link to explore the data set in the cellxgene application.
  • A new table, Single Cell RNA-Seq (cellxgene), appears in the Transcriptomics section of gene record pages with scRNA-Seq data. Rows represent individual data sets. Expand the rows to reveal static cellxgene images and links to dynamically view the data in the cellxgene application.

AlphaFold data available

  • Protein structure predictions from AlphaFold are now available in {{project}}. The AlphaFold database was downloaded and structures were associated to {{project}} genes via the UniProt ID. Jumper et al. 2021 and Varadi et al. 2022
  • The AlphaFold search returns all genes with AlphaFold predictions in {{project}}.
  • Gene pages include a table of AlphaFold data and the AlphaFold 3D viewer for dynamic visualization of the predicted structure.
