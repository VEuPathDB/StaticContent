New data in {{project}} 62

Genome sequence and annotation

  • Trypanosoma cruzi Berenice – Sequence was generated using a combination of Illumina short reads and MinION long reads and a hybrid assembly was produced de novo. Díaz-Viraqué et al. 2019 and GCA_013358655.1.

Proteomics

  • Adipose tissue vs bloodstream form proteomes – Proteomics comparison of adipose tissue and bloodstream forms of T brucei Lister 427. Quantification of protein synthesis employed L-Homopropargylglycine methods. Trindade et al. 2019.
  • Proteome during T brucei differentiation – Differential proteomics of T brucei differentiation with the in vitro system based on the inducible expression of RBP6. Doleželová et al. 2020.
  • Proteomic survey of the Leishmania major – High coverage proteome of Leishmania major Friedlin promastigotes Polanco et al. 2022.
  • Quantitative proteomic survey of the Leishmania major Friedlin and null mutants of LPG2 or FUT1. – High coverage proteomes of Leishmania major WT, Δlpg2- and Δfut1s. Polanco et al. 2022.

RNA Sequence

  • Metacyclic vs Procyclic form transcriptomes (2020) – RNA sequence transcriptomes of Trypanosoma brucei metacyclics obtained using an in vitro system compared to the non-infectious procyclic trypanosomes. Doleželová et al. 2020.

No new features in {{project}} 62
