We are pleased to announce the release of beta.VEuPathDB.org.

beta.VEuPathDB.org, is a unified database containing all data from all VEuPathDB-supported organisms.

  • This unified site currently supports 539 organisms with 460 annotated genome sequences and 79 unannotated genome assemblies.
  • You can search all VEuPathDB organisms or only a subset. Use the My Organisms Preferences tool to choose the subset of organisms you are interested in. My Organism Preferences Help
  • Comparative analyses between all taxa are easily accomplished. Run searches choosing organisms from diverse eukaryotic pathogens and their vectors and hosts such as the Apicomplexa, Trypanosomes, Fungi, Insects and Mammals.
  • For each gene, orthologs across all organisms are readily available in the gene page 'Orthologs and Paralogs within UniDB' table and the 'Transform by Orthology' tool in search strategies. Use this wide array of orthologous genes to understand the function of your genes of interest.
