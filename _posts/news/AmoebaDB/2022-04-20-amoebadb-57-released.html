New data in {{project}} 57

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Naegleria fowleri Ty – Isolated from a 1969 primary amebic meningoencephalitis (PAM) patient from Virginia, this organism belongs to genotype III. The genome sequences were assembled from Illumina HiSeq and PacBio sequencing data and verified with the optical mapping data for the identification of 37 contigs representing 37 chromosomes. Ali et al. 2021 and Joseph et al. 2021

Community Annotations Updated

We are pleased to announce the first update for an AmoebaDB genome through Apollo, a web-based genomic annotation editing platform. Changes in the genome annotation that were entered into Apollo by AmoebaDB users were reviewed by VEuPathDB annotation staff and transferred from Apollo into the VEuPathDB annotation track.
  • Acanthamoeba castellanii NEFF – We incorporated 180 changes, including creation of new genes, modification of existing genes, merges and splits. See a breakdown of the changes and the data incorporated.

New features in {{project}} 57

BLAST Tool Improvements

  • The BLAST (multi-query capable) tool, known previously as BLASTbeta, can perform queries with one or more FASTA-formatted sequences and offers many download types. The tool uses NCBI BLAST to find genes, genomic sequences, ESTs, or PopSet Sequences that have BLAST similarity to your input sequence. BLAST (multi-query capable) is available from the tools menu.
  • A new download option, 'This table (csv)', for the results of multi-sequence queries (combined result) retrieves the {{project}} result table for the results of multi-sequence queries (combined result).
  • View tutorial GIF.

Gene page sequence highlighting

  • Genomic and mRNA sequences on gene pages have a new color scheme. Introns are highlighted in yellow and UTR sequence are highlighted in pink. Previously they were gray and blue, respectively. Example
