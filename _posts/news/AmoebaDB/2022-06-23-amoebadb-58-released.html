Naegleria fowleri reference genome changed

  • Naegleria fowleri Ty now serves as the AmoebaDB reference for functional data originating from any strain of N fowleri. The Naegleria fowleri Ty genome was sequenced with PacBio, verified with the optical mapping data, and assembled into 37 contigs representing 37 chromosomes. Please Contact Us with questions or suggestions.

New data in {{project}} 58

Proteomics

  • Oxidized proteins in trophozoites adapted to auranofin (quantitative) – Oxidized proteome of Entamoeba histolytica HM-1:IMSS trophozoites adapted to auranofin, an agent cytotoxic to E. histolytica>. Shaulov et al. 2021.

Whole genome sequencing of isolates

  • N fowleri clinical and environmental WGS – Whole genome sequence of 52 Naegleria fowleri clinical and environmental isolates from around the world. The data were used to determine SNPs and CNV. Joseph et al. 2021.


New features in {{project}} 58

New actions for gene results

  • Save your search result as an ID list in My Data Sets. Open the 'Send To' dropdown menu and choose 'My Data Sets' to create a data set with the ID list of the search result. Your new data set record will appear on your My Data Sets page as a downloadable text file. You must be a registered {{project}} user and logged in to use this feature.
  • Transfer a gene list from {{project}} to VEuPathDB and analyze in context with all VEuPathDB organisms. From the {{project}} gene result, open the 'Send To' dropdown menu and choose 'VEuPathDB.org Strategy'. The VEuPathDB My Strategies page will open in a new window displaying a one step strategy that returns the gene list just transferred from {{project}}. Once transferred, use the 'Add a step' tool to extend the strategy with searches that can access data from all VEuPathDB organsims.
