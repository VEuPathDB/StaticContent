Naegleria fowleri reference genome changed

  • Naegleria fowleri Ty now serves as the AmoebaDB reference for functional data originating from any strain of N fowleri. This genome was sequenced with PacBio, verified with the optical mapping data and assembled into 37 contigs representing 37 chromosomes. Please Contact Us with questions or suggestions.

New data in {{project}} 58

Proteomics

  • Oxidized proteins in trophozoites adapted to auranofin (quantitative) – Oxidized proteome of Entamoeba histolytica HM-1:IMSS trophozoites adapted to auranofin, an agent cytotoxic to E. histolytica>. Shaulov et al. 2021.

Whole genome sequencing of isolates

  • N fowleri clinical and environmental WGS – Whole genome sequence of 52 Naegleria fowleri clinical and environmental isolates from around the world. The data were used to determine SNPs and CNV. Joseph et al. 2021.


New features in {{project}} 58

New 'Unannotated Intron Junctions' Search

  • The new Unannotated Intron Junctions search enables users to identify genes that contain, or are flanked by, unannotated high confidence intron junction-spanning reads from RNA-seq data. These genes may be incompletely or inaccurately annotated due to missing introns/exons and/or alternative splice variants. The search is categorized under Search for Genes, Function Prediction. See our PDF Tutorial or our Video Tutorial for more information.
