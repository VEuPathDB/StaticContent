New data in {{project}} 56

Genome Sequence

  • Entamoeba histolytica DS4-868 – This strain was isolated from a human diarrhea/dysentery patient and grown in axenic culture. The genome was sequenced using 454 (FLX fragment library) and Illumina methods to a genome coverage of 24X coverage. PRJNA51259
  • Entamoeba histolytica KU48 – This strain was isolated from the aspirate of a human liver absess and grown in axenic culture. The genome was sequenced using 454 (FLX fragment library) and Illumina methods to a genome coverage of 44.2X coverage PRJNA51235

RNA Sequence

  • Auranofin-adapted trophozoite (AFAT) transcriptome – RNA-seq transcriptome of Entamoeba histolytica HM-1:IMSS wild type trophozoites and trophozoites adapted to auranofin, an agent cytotoxic to E. histolytica. Shaulov et al. 2021

New features in {{project}} 56

BLAST Tool Improvements

  • Our BLAST (multi-query capable) tool is the new standard. Known previously as BLASTbeta, BLAST (multi-query capable) can perform queries with one or more FASTA-formatted sequences and offers many download types. Our tool uses NCBI BLAST to find genes, genomic sequences, ESTs, or PopSet Sequences that have BLAST similarity to your input sequence. The single BLAST tool has been removed.
  • There is a new download option for the results of multi-sequence queries (combined result). The new 'This table (csv)' download option retrieves the {{project}} result table for the results of multi-sequence queries (combined result).
