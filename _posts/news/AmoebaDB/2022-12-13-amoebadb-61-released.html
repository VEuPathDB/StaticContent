2023 VEuPathDB Annual Workshop is In-Person

Join us at the The University of Georgia for the 16th Annual VEuPathDB workshop on May 15 – 19th, 2023. During the workshop, short lectures will precede hands-on exercises where discussion and conversation are encouraged. The objective is for attendees to leave with an understanding of the breadth of data available, how to effectively use VEuPathDB resources to mine the available data, and to use preconfigured workflows to analyze unintegrated data. Attendance is limited to 30 people. Registration, housing, most meals are free during the workshop. Attendees are expected to pay for travel and some dinners.

New features in {{project}} 61

Associated Data Sets

  • Data sets that come from the same publication are now linked. The new 'Associated Data Sets' section on any data set record page will link to other data from the same publication within {{project}} as well as data from organisms supported in different VEuPathDB sites. View tutorial GIF.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
